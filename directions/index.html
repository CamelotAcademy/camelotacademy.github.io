Directions

Camelot Academy

809 Proctor St, Durham, NC 27707

Directions from I-40 East

  1. Follow I-40 West and use the right 2 lanes to take exit 279B to merge onto NC-147 N toward Durham/Downtown
  2. Exit onto S Duke St in Durham.
  3. Turn left onto Jackson St.
  4. Turn left onto Vickers Ave.
  5. Turn right onto Proctor St.
  6. Camelot Academy will be on the left (809 Proctor St)

Directions from I-85 North

  1. Follow I-85 North and use the right 2 lanes to take exit 172 for NC-147 S toward Durham/Research Triangle Park/Downtown
  2. Take exit 13 for Chapel Hill St.
  3. Turn left onto W Chapel Hill St.
  4. Turn right onto William Vickers Ave.
  5. Continue onto Jackson St.
  6. Turn right onto Vickers St.
  7. Turn right onto Proctor St.
  8. Camelot Academy will be on the left (809 Proctor St)
