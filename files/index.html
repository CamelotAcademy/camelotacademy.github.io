Hamlet: A Televised Tragedy - Cast of Characters
Hamlet: A Televised Tragedy - Script

Announcing auditions for 'Hamlet: A Televised Tragedy' (formerly 'Elsinore Now!) open to all Camelot students, parents, faculty & staff. Auditions to be held next Wednesday and Thursday (1/29-30) from 3:30 to 5:30. Consult the Cast of Characters for a brief description of the roles being cast and the scene(s) in which those characters appear. Consult the script to familiarize yourself with the scene and the character's lines (no need to memorize yet! we will be reading from scripts).

Please email me (Scott@CamelotAcademy.org) if there is a character you would like to audition for. Please indicate in your email: a) the character(s) you would like to read for and the day(s) you are available (Wednesday only, Thursday only, either Wednesday or Thursday) this will help us to organize the auditions more efficiently.
