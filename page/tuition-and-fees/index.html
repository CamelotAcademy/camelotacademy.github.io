Tuition and Fees

2017-2018 School Year


Camelot Academy has a tiered tuition system. The following rates apply to the 2018-2019 school year:
 
KinderMAPP (KinderMAPP/1st*): $10,900.
MAPP (Multi-Age Progressive Primary) (1st-5th*): $12,850.
Middle Grades (5th-8th*): $13,850.
Upper School (8th-12th*): $14,850.
 
* Camelot classes are multi-age/multi-grade based on students' individual needs, hence grades served at each division overlap.

In addition to tuition, there is a one-time, New Student Enrollment Fee of $800 and semester fees (there are two semesters) which range, depending on grade level, from $600 to $1,850 per semester.

Re-enrolling students pay a $300 re-enrollment fee to hold their student's place for the coming year.

Tuition may be paid in one, two, or four installments. Families who wish to set up a payment plan over a longer period may use tuitionsolution.com. Discounts are available for this option. Families who enroll a second child at Camelot Academy enjoy a 5% sibling discount.

Tuition rates for the upcoming school year are posted in January.
