Weekly Update for 01/27/2019

Sunday, January 27, 2019

Greetings!
 
It was a really big week at Camelot!
 
•   We welcomed two new students - Scarlett Bresnahan to Middle Grades and Cordi Ramont to Jr MAPP!
•   Our Basketball Knights wrapped up their season with both a win and a cliff hanger tournament game.  Congratulations to Coach Carlos and the team (Claire Rusconi-Warner, David Madugu, Harry Jiang, Ian Powrie, Jack Lu, John Wang, Kevin Cui, LM Fountain, Mack Taylor, Pengyu Qian, Travis Fountain, Zach Ireland and David Farber)
•   Our FTC Robotics team competed in the Qualifying Tournament in Farmville, NC.  Congrats to CoachesChris Larsen, John Ireland and Robyn Davis Stephens along with volunteers Michelle Banal, Angela Ireland and Chris Bailey. The team (Johnson Zheng, Zigy Lim, Max Terry, Eli Terry, Xander Lord, Kathleen Stancil-Sutton, Zachary Ireland, Ashley Hartman and Heather Hartman) won the 1st Place Connect Award and the 2nd Place Motivate Award.
•   Our Ethics Class competed in the NC High School Ethics Bowl. Congrats to Mr. Tom and class membersNick Johnson, William Whetzel, Mack Taylor, Harry Jones, David Farber, Alina Junrong, and PengyuQian for providing excellent representation of our school’s first participation in this event.
•   Our Upper School Intro to Digital Media Technology elective class launched under the direction of Mr. Irfan
•   Astronaut Don Thomas visited MAPP and Middle Grades students and gave an inspiring presentation

Reminders:   
We are very excited to launch our application season for the Global Studies Certificate Program! This two-year program is for rising 10th and 11th graders in the 2019-20 school year. As we face an increasingly interconnected world, students need to develop knowledge and skills that will help them be true global citizens. We have created a program to help students find extra opportunities to do just that. Successful completion will earn students a certificate and special transcript notation. The program has various requirements, such as academic achievements, community service, cultural exposure activities and leadership milestones. Applications and additional information are available here: camelotacademy.org/global-studies. Applications are due February 12, 2019 and interviews for all applicants are tentatively scheduled for February 22. Contact Ms. Charlotte at charlotte@camelotacademy.org with any questions.
 
 
Parents - please be on the lookout for an email from Ms. Jill this week to schedule your February Family Conference.  All MAPP, Middle Grades and Juniors will have a conference.  Additional Upper School conferences are scheduled by teacher or parent request.
