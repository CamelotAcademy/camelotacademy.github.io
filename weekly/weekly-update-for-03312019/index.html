Weekly Update for 03/31/2019

Sunday, March 31, 2019

Greetings!
 
I’m sorry to have to share the very sad news that last week Class of ‘16 alum Sarah Fauver passed away unexpectedly.  As we come to terms with this news, we will seek ways to support our students and teachers, and to remember Sarah and commemorate her life.  I share this with you now so your family can talk through this together in advance of school tomorrow. Rita and I will be available to see people, as needed, and we will do what family does – work through life’s challenges together.  Here is a link to Sarah’s obituary: https://www.legacy.com/obituaries/hartfordcourant/obituary.aspx?n=sarah-m-fauver&pid=192036200
 
Following is the rest of the rest of the Weekly Update.
