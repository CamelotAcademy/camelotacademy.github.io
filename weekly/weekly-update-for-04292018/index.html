Weekly Update for 04/29/2018

Sunday, April 29, 2018

Camelot Update - April 29, 2018

Greetings!
 
We had as full a week as can be imagined.  After wrapping up the Alpha schedule, we spent three mornings taking standardized tests.  Meanwhile, our youngest members were off on trips with Ms. Robyn and Coach Carlos that ranged from delivering Meals on Wheels in the big bus to learning all about lemurs and fire fighting.  Our juniors explored college campuses with Mr. Matt and our seniors prepared in earnest for their final month of high school with picture taking, speech writing and Master of Ceremony rehearsals. We had an afternoon Talent Show that moved us from laughter to wistful tears, and on Thursday we celebrated the return of the SUN with a great Spring Festival.  Our performing groups immortalized their work at the recording studio, while the teachers and I did a first read-through of Mr. Scott’s terrific graduation skit.  The athletes celebrated their year with a cookout at the farm yesterday and today the Robotics teams will have their end-of-year celebration.  
 
 
Congratulations to...
