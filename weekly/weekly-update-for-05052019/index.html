Weekly Update for 05/05/2019

Sunday, May 05, 2019

Greetings!

  

Thanks for your participation on Friday night to make Evening of the Arts a joyous occasion.  It is always a special treat to celebrate the artistic talents of our Camelot Family - and this year was extra special in extending that celebration to include our Multi-Generational Band.  Kudos to Ms. Judith for the design of our 2018-19 School Shirt.  We were “in full bloom” wearing them on stage together!  Thanks to Mr. Irfan for the professional recording of the event. DVD’s may be purchased for $20. Order info (and great picture!) is here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfebQhq98tv3Y54bXh5snOWf9BeDp4NI-lEjWq6DXCKXwjcpA/viewform?usp=sf_link

In other artistic news, MAPP students finished their last collaborative art project for the year, which is on display in the stairwell. They read The Bee Tree by Patricia Polacco and created a scene from the book.  Students did a wonderful job painting, planning, and organizing the way items would be displayed. They used mixed media and multiple techniques to achieve their final design - check it out!

Reminders/Announcements:

 

This week is National Teacher Appreciation Week!  We certainly have much to appreciate and celebrate.

 

Let’s Fly - Edu-Ventures Summer Camp with Ms. Amanda and Ms. Jenny. June 17-June 21.

Students will discover the relationships of lift, thrust, drag, and weight to aviation.  We will explore these scientific principles and related mathematical formulas through a series of hands-on experiments and daily field trips. We will also learn about the history of human flight from Leonardo da Vinci to the Wright Brothers, and beyond. Field trips will include: a visit to RDU airport flight deck, a private airstrip, and a trip to Woodcroft pool, where we will experiment with handmade pontoon planes.

Register here: https://www.camelotacademy.org/page/summer-programs/
