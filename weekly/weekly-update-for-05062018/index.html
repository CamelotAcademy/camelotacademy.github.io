Sunday, May 06, 2018

Camelot Update - May 6, 2018

Greetings!
 
Thanks to everyone for the great turnout on Friday night for Evening of the Arts.  It was a joy to behold the talents of our young people and to celebrate our wonderful community.  The evening was a lovely finale to a super-charged first week of X Schedule.  Our students explored the stars, painted garden scenes, dissected frogs, visited cutting-edge business start-up hubs, interviewed a state senator and learned about organic farming.  
 
Congratulations to...
 
Ms. Alison and her husband Cory on the birth of Leona Aviva Sherman.
 
 
Thanks to...
 
....Not only all of our arts teachers for Friday night (Mr. Glenn, Ms. Roni, Ms. Alison, Ms. Judith and Ms. Liz) but also to Mr. Dan for the tech work (and Michael Berro and Mr. Josh for helping sub for him), and to the team of people who stepped in to see that the “show went on - well” in Ms. Alison’s absence - most especially Ms. Erin, Ms. Liz, Ms. Amanda, Ms. Rachel...and Mr. Glenn, who has now proven his talent with elementary students!
 
...Ms. Judith for the amazing T-Shirt design we will all enjoy wearing for quite some time to come.
 
...Middle Grades student Catherine Williams, who won 2nd prize in the NC Museum of History Literature Contest for a script she wrote entitled the Spanish Flu The Spanish Lady.  She researched the topic and wrote this script for her X Schedule Middle School Social Studies class in January.
