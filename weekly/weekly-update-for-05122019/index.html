Edu Ventures - Week of July 8-12 - NATURAL WONDERS
With Ms. Nicole and Ms. Amanda - at the Camelot Farm!
Campers will participate in the animal care routines of our goats, pigs, chickens and ducks by providing food and water, gathering and packaging eggs, etc. Daily hikes will include a specific focus that will inform our language arts (opinion writing and narratives) and math lessons (graphing and measuring).  We will learn how to fish, build forts and shelters, tend the garden and use our detective skills to identify plant and wildlife.
Info and sign up here:  https://www.camelotacademy.org/page/summer-programs/
