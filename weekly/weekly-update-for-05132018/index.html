Camelot Update - May 13, 2018

Greetings!

 

Happy Mother’s Day to all the mothers and “women who mother” in our Camelot Family.  I hope the day provides you an opportunity to reflect and appreciate the difference your love makes in the world.

 

Picture of Baby Geese that just hatched at the farm!

 

We are in the final two weeks of this school year and there is much to keep track of as we move towards our big finale on Saturday, May 26. Thank you for your attention to the details!

 

Our X Schedule this week took our students to Wilmington, Charlotte, Greensboro, and the Harris Nuclear plant, among other places. In addition, thirteen of our Entrepreneurship/Non-Profit students had week-long job shadow placements at sites of interest.  Our Morning Meetings were brimming over with all the stories to be shared.

...Senior Carson Blythe on being awarded an 18K Elon Engagement Scholarship.

 

...the MAPP classes for the publication of “I Rode My Fox to Antarctica.” Kudos to Ms. Amanda for orchestrating this publication.
