Sunday, May 20, 2018

Camelot Update - May 20, 2018

Greetings!
 
We have arrived at the final week of the year! There are still exams to take, projects to complete, yearbooks to enjoy and graduation festivities to coordinate. We look forward to a joyous celebration together next weekend.
                                                                                                                                                                          
Our summer programs start on June 11 — there is limited space in Edu-Ventures, Summer School and our teen programs.  See below for details.
 
Congratulations to the Robofoxes Robotics Team (Anna Bailey, Clarabelle Brown, Nate Hopkins, Patrick Ireland, Ellie Larsen, Ethan Salemy and Coach Robyn, Coach Angela and Ms. Michelle), who have just finished competing at the FIRST Razorback Invitational Competition in Arkansas.  We are awaiting the final results this morning….
 
Thanks to...
Our X period part-time teachers who have added great learning value to these weeks - Ms. Patricia (Math), Ms. Beatrice (Mandarin) and Ms. Maribel (Spanish).  We cannot thank them enough for their dedication to the progress of our students.  We look forward to working with them again in the future.

Summer Programs!
