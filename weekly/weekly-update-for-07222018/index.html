Weekly Update for 07/22/2018

Sunday, July 22, 2018

Greetings and Happy New Year!
 
This is the first of my regular Update emails for the 2018-19 school year.  A special welcome to our new families!  It was wonderful to see so many of you at the pool party.
 
The summer is passing very quickly and in just three weeks the teachers will be returning.  The energy and creativity going into the new year is at a particular high.  We are very excited to share everything from building enhancements to new programming with you in the coming weeks.  Here are some important reminders about info that is relevant now:
 
•    All rising 3rd-12th graders should be past the halfway point in the Summer Reading (AR) requirements.  The building is open each day from 8 AM until 5:30 PM for drop-in computer tests.  The final deadline for completion is Friday, August 17.
 
•    Please check out the online 2018-19 school calendar found on our new website here: http://www.camelotacademy.org/calendar/ .  Many thanks to parent Dain Anderson for his amazing work creating our new site for the new school year!  We welcome your feedback and ideas as we continue to enhance the info and features.
 
•    School supply lists can also be found on our new website here:   http://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/ .
 
•    Ms. Michelle sent an email approximately a week ago with details about many of the overnight trips planned for Middle and Upper School students this school year - everything from Charlottesville, Virginia to Florence, Italy is in the works!
