Weekly Update for 08/19/2018

Sunday, August 19, 2018

Greetings!
 
I hope this email finds you and yours doing well and ready for the new school year.  The teachers returned last Monday and we had a jam-packed week of meetings, moving and senior interviews. We welcome to our staff my daughter, Rita Glynn, as our full time counselor and Angela “Frankie” Entzel as our environmental/farm educator.  We look forward to introducing them to everyone at Parent Orientation tomorrow night.
 
As you are aware, Parent Orientation this year is for ALL families - we have many exciting new activities to warrant such a gathering.  Due to very limited space, this is a parent-only event. We will provide child care for youngsters who have been signed up in advance (today, please) at this link:  http://www.signupgenius.com/go/10C0845A8AF22ABF49-camelot1.
 
Please note that we are asking parents to arrive early enough to allow for parking and completing paperwork IN ADVANCE of the meeting start times (see below).  Our parking lot will fill early, but there will be attendants on duty to direct you to nearby street parking.
 
Also, all families considering having a student participate in one of our athletic teams should be present for the 7:15 PM athletics meeting (between the Middle and Upper School orientation times).  The discount deadline to sign up for teams for the year is Wednesday, August 22.  Soccer and Cross country practices begin the week of August 27. Details about all of our athletics offerings can be found here: https://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/ under Forms and Handouts.
 
The Middle and Upper School trip to Charlottesville, Virginia has a final registration deadline of Tuesday, September 4.  Info here:
Fall 2018: Charlottesville, Va -- Ready for sign up!
