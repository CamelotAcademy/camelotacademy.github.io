Weekly Update for 08/26/2018

Sunday, August 26, 2018

Greetings!
 
We had a wonderful opening week, thanks to the efforts of every single member of Team Camelot, our families and our students. The idyllic weather and fun activities made the adjustment back to alarm clocks much easier.  We welcomed our new students, revisited our community guidelines and planted the seeds for our theme ofBloom. We are now well-situated to begin our academic agenda on Monday.
 
As you are aware, the coming week is REVIEW WEEK.  In the coming days, students will review material in all of their classes as a “warm up” prior to placement/ pre-testing.  During the coming week we will do more whole group instruction than the norm - including a few things that might be too simple or too challenging for some students.  Please help us assure your student that this is all part of the process of finding “just the right fit” - much like the Goldilocks story!
 
There are many, many calls to action to get everything launched.  Please note the following:

Music Opportunities:

•    Folks of all ages interested in the Multi-Generational Band should come to an info meeting/first session (with instruments or voices!) on Wednesday, September 5 from 4-5:30 PM.  If you are interested in participating but cannot make this day/time please email me.
