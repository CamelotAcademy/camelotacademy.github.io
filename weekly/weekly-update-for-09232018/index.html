Weekly Update for 09/23/2018

Sunday, September 23, 2018

Greetings!
 
Thanks for the great participation in Upper School Parent Night.  We look forward to welcoming MAPP and Middle Grades parents this Thursday evening.
 
This coming week is Club Preview Week.  Students may stay after school to try out one of our many offerings at the regular after-school rate. Sign-up for the full semester is due by this Friday. Please note that students joining the Tuesday FLL Robotics team will be transported to The North Carolina School of Science and Math after school, provided we have their sign-up in advance. You can check out all the options here: https://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/.
 
Family conferences are the week of October 8.  Please be on the lookout for an email from Ms. Jill with the link to sign up.  Your prompt response is requested.
 
Our NHS Chapter is coordinating donations for Hurricane Florence relief through Operation Airdrop. Please bring your donations of first aid supplies, canned food, can openers, AA batteries (in closed packages), toiletries, cleaning supplies, and flashlights to the plastic collection bin in the Big Hall.
