Weekly Update for 09/30/2018

Sunday, September 30, 2018

Greetings!
 
CONGRATULATIONS to Senior Kendall Silva on her acceptance to Millsaps College with a $112,000 merit scholarship!
 
Thanks for another great turnout at Parent Night this week.  Also, thanks for your many donations for the NHS Hurricane Relief efforts.
 
We have had many requests to see pictures of the six baby pigs born at our farm last Sunday morning.  Many of our students have seen them in person this week and many Sr. MAPP students took stock of their first five days of growth yesterday - they range in weight from 2.5 to 3.5 pounds.
Here you go!  Enjoy!  https://23photography.pixieset.com/piglets/
 
We have wrapped up Module 1 and are now embarking on our first Minimester Week, which will be followed by a week of Family Conferences.  The Charlottesville trip departs this morning and our first-ever musical begins rehearsal tomorrow.  Exciting times!
 
IMPORTANT:  ALL Middle Grades and Upper School students are to come to school each day this week with assignment notebooks, math texts and notebooks, Accelerated Reader books and materials needed to work on Academic Fair.  We look forward to an individually-tailored Minimester experience for everyone, with a good balance of learning, exploration and service.
 
Costume and Prop Donations Needed - please send on Monday, if possible!
 

  • Spools of thread, sewing machine needles, sewing pins, extra plastic bobbins, elastic, seam splitters, fabric in quantities of a yard or more  
 
  • Floor-length prom dresses or bridesmaid dresses that you do not want returned - preferably sizes 4-10
