Weekly Update for 10/07/2018

Sunday, October 07, 2018

Greetings and Happy October!
 
Our Middle and Upper School students wrapped up a week of Minimester that included a trip to Charlottesville, VA, rehearsals for the upcoming musical, set and costume preparation, SAT/PSAT preparation, Academic Fair work, individual writing conferences, multiple service trips, a Spanish-class trip to the Nasher, a global studies presentation and PE outings that ranged from hiking to swimming.  Many thanks to Ms. Amy for her assistance with the scheduling and to seniors and NHS officers Kira Arges and Sean Kowis, who were instrumental in arranging the service trips.
 
Kudos to our Cross Country team - and their coaches Mr. Tom and Ms. Magda - for wrapping up a great season this week. This group of young people were exemplary in their support of each other and represented our school with honor. Congrats to Hardit Bhutani, Lean Cron, Kevin Cui, David Farber, William Harrison, Nate Hopkins, Seth Hurley, Patrick Ireland, Ellie Larsen, Jackson McCalmon, Dylan McGlinn, Jacob Pinto, Ethan Salemy, Kendall Sliva, Mack Taylor, Jackie Wenzlik, William Whetzel and Isabel Williams.
 
Now our attention will turn to Family Conference Week - an incredibly important time for students, parents and teachers to review the plan for the year and progress to date.  Kudos to Ms. Jill, who has worked tirelessly the past two weeks to put together the complicated schedule that brings all the players together!  Here are a few conference reminders:
•   Please arrive a few minutes early to review student work before the conference
•   Please take some time to organize your thoughts and questions prior to the meeting so we can use your allotted time for maximum advantage
•   Please be sure your Middle or Upper School student is in attendance - they are required participants in the process
