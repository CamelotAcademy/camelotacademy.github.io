Weekly Update for 10/28/2018

Sunday, October 28, 2018

Greetings!
 
We just passed the Mid-Semester mark of the first semester and are approaching the time of year when calendar pages seem to fly by.  Friday was as soggy as the forecast predicted; we are hoping for a drier end to the coming week so we can enjoy Fall Festival together.  More info about all of this below.
 
Service Projects:
 
There are two student-driven service projects in the works - Socktober and the post-Halloween Candy Drive.
 
 
Socktober is Here Once More! (By Xander Lord)
 
It's that time of year again, Socktober!  Socktober is an annual sock and can food collection drive that the National Honor Society and the Robotics Team run at Camelot. We have learned that many homeless shelters are well stocked on many types of clothing such as shirts and pants but are actually rather low on socks to give to the less fortunate. We are taking donations of any kind of sock as long as they are not pre-worn and are in their original packaging. We are also taking canned goods and bottled water as long as they are unopened and undamaged to give to the victims of Hurricane Florence.  Send in your packages of socks and can foods with your student so they can donate them to their homeroom collection box. The homeroom that collects the most socks, cans, and bottles by the end of Socktober will win a pizza party. The drive is taking place from now until the Academic Fair (November 16). 
 
Candy Drive (by Nina Elvin)
 
As you know, this Wednesday is Halloween. We are pleased to tell you that Camelot will be holding a candy drive to raise money to donate to hurricane relief funds. Candy collection will take place from November 1st – 6th and we are accepting all individually-wrapped candy. We will give the candy to Candy Buy Back, a program that will give us $1 per pound. Candy Buy Back will then send the candy to troops overseas and the money we raise will be donated to the American Red Cross hurricane relief. Please send your student to school with any leftover Halloween candy you wish to donate to the cause.
