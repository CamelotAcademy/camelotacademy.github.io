Weekly Update for 11/04/2018

Sunday, November 4, 2018

Greetings!
 
I hope you are enjoying the extra hour of weekend on this pretty November day. We embarked on the weekend with a wonderful Fall Festival - spearheaded by the Whetzel and Benson-Sliva families with MUCH support from parents, staff, students and friends.  It is hard to capture the gratitude in my heart as I watched the fun and community of our gathering.  Thank you to everyone for helping to create such a lovely memory.
 
In less than two weeks we will gather again to celebrate the research work of many of our young people and to enjoy camaraderie.  We ask that EVERYONE return the Academic Fair/Thanksgiving Feast RSVP (orange flyers) that went home last week.  If you are in need of another copy you can find it here – under “Forms and Handouts”:
https://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/
 
Congratulations to senior Kendall Sliva on her acceptance to Southwestern University (TX) with a $108,000 Ruter Scholar Award and her acceptance to Emory and Henry College (VA) with an 80K Founder’s Scholarship.
