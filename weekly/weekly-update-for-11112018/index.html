Weekly Update for 11/11/2018

Sunday, November 11, 2018

Greetings!
 
I hope this email finds you well and looking forward to our Friday festivities.  Thanksgiving has always been my favorite holiday - sharing it with our Camelot Family is a highlight of the year. Middle and Upper School students will be presenting their research projects; MAPP, Middle and Upper School vocal students will be performing.  In addition, the multi-generational band will make its debut!  Later this week you will receive an email with the program of events so you will know what time your student will be presenting/performing.
 
On Wednesday and Thursday of this week we will welcome two colleagues from our accrediting organization - the National Independent Private School Association (NIPSA).  We look forward to sharing our students’ accomplishments and our school with these visitors.
 
 
Reminders:
- It is the Last Week of Socktober! (By Xander Lord)
We are coming up on the end of Socktober!  Send in your packages of new socks and canned foods with your student so they can donate them to their homeroom collection box. We are also taking donations at Academic Fair on Friday, November 16th! The homeroom that collects the most will win a pizza party. Remember, this is the last week of Socktober, so now is the time to donate.
 
- Mr. Mike is recruiting Saturday Parent Service leaf rakers - please contact him at Mike@camelotacademy.org.
