Weekly Update for 12/09/2018

Sunday, December 09, 2018

Greetings and Happy Early Winter!
 
Congratulations to Senior Kendall Sliva on her acceptance to Hendrix College with a 104 K Merit Scholarship.
 
Kudos to the FLL Robofoxes (Ethan Salemy, Nate Hopkins, Patrick Ireland, Clarabelle Brown, Anna Bailey, Ellie Larsen and Coaches Robyn, Angela and Michelle) who won the Champion’s Award at yesterday’s regional qualifier event in Goldsboro.  The team will now move on to compete at the state level competition.
 
We had another adventure-filled week of X Schedule. Student trips ranged from cycling adventures to meeting with the Mexican and Guatemalan Consulates.  Many thanks to parents Jenny Terry, Ami Goldstein and Rula Mouawad, who lent their talents and time to help make our programming a success.
