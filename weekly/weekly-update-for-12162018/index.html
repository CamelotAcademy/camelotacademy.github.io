Weekly Update for 12/16/2018

Sunday, December 16, 2018

Greetings and Happy End of 2018!
 
I hope that your family made some nice winter memories this week.  Our community did a great job re-entering on Wednesday and re-setting priorities based on the interruption.  It works out well that we have the upcoming half-week to bring the calendar year to a close.
 
Congratulations to Senior Kendall Sliva, who was accepted to Berry College with a 72K Berry Academic Scholarship and to the Honors Program at Ohio Wesleyan University with a 120K Branch Rickey Scholarship.
 
Congratulations to Senior Sean Kowis who was accepted to High Point University with a 40K Presidential Fellowship.
 
Kudos to the Flaming Mechanical Coyotes Robotics Team (Michael Mouawad, Guillaume Preud’homme, Lincoln Smith, Gavin Petelle, Jackson McCalmon, Hardit Bhutani, Simon Holt and Donald Hite), who competed in the Regional FLL qualifier in Charlotte yesterday.  Many thanks to Coaches Robyn, Angela and Michelle, and parents Jonathon Holt, Jack McCalmon and Manpreet Bhutani for making it a great experience for all.
 
Way to go, Multi-Generational Band, who performed their first “public” appearance yesterday at Northgate Mall!  We look forward to sharing a few of their holiday selections at our Wednesday Morning Meeting.
 
 
This upcoming short week has several holiday and “make-up” events:
 
 - Spirit Week Reboot 
Monday - Pajama Day
Tuesday - Twin Day (Dress up like your friend or teacher)
 
- Holidays Around the World - we are looking forward to our annual Morning Meeting tradition of parent-sharing of holiday traditions
 
- Holiday Beaded Gift Sale – message from students Isabel Williams and Robert Sutcliffe
As part of Camelot Academy’s multiple traditions, each year the school receives beautifully handcrafted, beaded ornaments made from a Zimbabwean artist. We will be selling some of Charles’ artwork at lunchtime this week. 100% of proceeds go directly back to the artist. The prices to purchase ornaments range from $3-$30. Please feel free to contact Isabel Williams, Robert Sutcliffe or Ms. Lori if you would like more information!
 
The Upcoming Week:
 
Monday
Games Club make-up session
 
Tuesday
Last day of X Schedule for First Semester
 
Wednesday
School Closed at 12:30 PM for Winter Break
 
 
December 20 - January 4 - Winter Break
 
January 7 - Classes Resume
 
 I will send the next installment of the Weekly Update on Sunday, January 6, 2019!!!!!!
 
 
I send you and your family heartfelt wishes for a wonderful break and a very Happy New Year.
 
Best,

Thelma
