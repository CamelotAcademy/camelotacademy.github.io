Weekly Update for 09/01/2019

Sunday, September 01, 2019

Greetings!

 

Thank you for helping to make this a great first week for our students.  We enjoyed sharing a meal with you at Parent Orientation on Monday, and it was lovely to have so many family members participate in the Summer Book Conversations on Friday.

 

During Student Orientation, our young people had a nice time making new friends, reflecting on our theme of LENSES, learning the reasons behind our policies, and settling in. The feedback from the faculty was that just when they thought the first two days couldn’t be topped, Friday set an even higher bar of student engagement!  Many thanks to Ms. Lori, Ms. Michelle and Ms. Nicole for orchestrating these days on behalf of Upper, Middle and MAPP students.

 

When we return from the holiday weekend on Tuesday, we will enter a six-day period of review and pre-testing. As you will recall, material at this time may seem too hard or too easy - that is part of the process.  Please remind your student that although pre-assessments are important for a productive year, they are not a factor in grades and should not be a source of stress.

 

Our First LEGO League (FLL) robotics team is getting ready to launch.  Families of students ages 9-14 will receive an email from Ms. Jill on Tuesday with registration info. Practices will be on Tuesdays and Saturdays, with a first meeting on Tuesday, September 10.

 

Seeking used materials:

The MAPP classrooms are seeking a few used IPADS for classroom use.  If you have an old tablet you are willing to part with and wipe clean, it would be greatly appreciated.

 

Our environmental/outdoors classes are gearing up for work and are seeking the following used tools:

6 Spade Shovels, 10 Hoes, 2 Flat Shovels, 2 hand saws, 2 Adzes
