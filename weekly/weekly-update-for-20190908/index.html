Weekly Update for 09/08/2019

Sunday, September 08, 2019

Greetings!

 

We had a great first week of classes.  There were many golden nuggets of student engagement and sharing that are the hallmarks of what makes our school special. You could hear the squeals of delight coming from the MAPP classrooms as they had their sharing time and explorations.  Middle and Upper School students had some amazing discussions in the Digital Citizenship classes, as well as in the screening of the first part of the 2E movie.  We want to share the resources with you:

-         Digital Citizenship info:   https://www.commonsense.org/education/Link

-         2E= Twice Exceptional (Gifted +) resources:  http://2emovie.com/

(Look for an email invite this week to a parent screening of this movie)

 

Kudos to our administrative team - Wendy, Jill, Scott, Amy, Leah and Michael Berro, who have been juggling the myriad of details and paperwork needed to launch our programming each year.  It is amazing how many “little things” there are that go into the Big Picture and each of these colleagues has played “catch” to a lot of items that are rarely noticed - unless they get dropped!  :)

 

 

As you are aware, the upcoming week is a short week for students and a very full week for staff as we engage in our Faculty Retreat.  Each student has been provided a Retreat Feedback Form for you to complete and return on Monday.  In the event that the form has been misplaced, you can find a copy of it here:

https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2019/Faculty_Retreat_Form_2019.pdf

We appreciate your support of this pivotal time we take to review student pre-test information to create individualized learning plans.
