Weekly Update for 09/22/2019

Sunday, September 22, 2019

Greetings!

 

We have had a very successful first week of X Schedule.  Our MAPP students and Environmental students have had adventures at the farm, while others have cooked, painted, and climbed through their own journeys. AP Computer Science students have sunk their teeth into some challenging topics, and Global Studies students have learned a lot about Central and South America in readiness for upcoming meetings and excursions.  Middle Grades students have embarked on a study of local history and culture with outings every day. Perhaps the highlight for me came on Wednesday evening when three teachers - debriefing with me separately - commented on adjusting their courses to the pacing of the students and the flexibility allowed by the time – and the magic moments that followed.  An important goal of our X Schedule is for teachers and students to learn how to find that learning flow and sustain it - we are well on the way.

 

This was also a big week for athletics at Camelot.  Our Soccer Knights played two games and were thrilled to win the Tuesday game vs. Fletcher Academy.  Our Cross Country Knights competed in a meet we hosted on Friday. Several of the students new to the team completed their first 5K, while many returning runners set new personal bests. Many thanks to the parent and student volunteers who made this endeavor possible.

 

The Week Ahead is Club Preview Week.  Please refer to the email you received on Friday from Ms. Wendy - here https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2019/2019_Fall_Club_Signup.pdf - for all of the details.  As a reminder, this week students may visit any club without committing to the semester.  Please note - actual sign-up is due Friday, September 27 (Except for the band, which has an extension until October 4.)
