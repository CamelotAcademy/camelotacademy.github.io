Weekly Update for 10/06/2019

Sunday, October 06, 2019

Greetings and Happy October!

 

We have had a smooth ride into our A-E Schedule; teachers and students have had a very good week.  As the month of October unfolds, it brings with it a lot of activity - please tune in to these Updates to guide you through!  Here we go….

 

This Wednesday is a fundraising dinner to benefit our FTC Robotics Team.  Here is a message from Senior Zach Ireland:

"The RoboKnights have partnered with City Barbecue, a restaurant close to Southpoint Mall, to raise funds for the team. We are having a fundraiser at their restaurant on October 9th. The funds raised will go to our overall budget as well as to our scholarship program for team members. To participate in this fundraiser, bring this flyer( https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2019/city_bbq_fundraiser_coupon.png ) to show to the cashier when you check out. You must present the flyer to have your purchase count towards our fundraiser. The address for City Barbecue is 208 West N Carolina 54, Durham, NC, 27713. We hope to see you all there on October the 9th! Thanks again!"

 

On Thursday, we host our Middle Grades/Upper School Parent Night.  Parents will go through their student’s schedule and learn about expectations and protocols of each class.  Because this communication is pivotal to our success with your youngster, this is a required parent event.

School closed for everyone at 3:30 PM

Optional Care available for MAPP and Middle Grades students, by advance reservation

- 5 PM Optional parent screening of the 2e Movie (http://2emovie.com/) and pizza dinner.  Sign-Up Link is here:  https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10c0a44a4ab2faaf94-mgus

- 6 PM (prior to our Upper School Parent Night)- Info Meeting about Spanish Immersion Trip to Ecuador

A Spanish Immersion Trip to Ecuador will be led by Ms. Charlotte in June 2020! Students will learn through homestays, local school visits, community service and a full Spanish course credit. Planned highlights include visits to the equator, colonial Quito, the ruins of Cochasquí, Otavalo craft market, Cuicocha crater lake and much more! This trip is for students 15 and older who will have completed Spanish 2 and higher in June. Trip dates are June 7 - July 4, 2020. Contact Ms. Charlotte at charlotte@camelotacademy.org with any questions and to RSVP.

 

-6:30-8:30 PM Parent Night for Middle and Upper School - please allow time to find parking (neighborhood streets) and be inside by 6:30!

 

 

On Friday, we host our annual Fall Festival for students and parents.  Admission is free and includes games, races, crafts, karaoke and fun!  We are excited to share our new spaces and projects with parents - including our week-old piglets! The Cilantro food truck will be on site for those who wish to bring money or credit card to buy food.  Parent Service Opportunity – we need baked goods for the Cake Walk!  Please sign up here:

https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10c0a4eafa72aa7fd0-20183
