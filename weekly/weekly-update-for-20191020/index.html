Weekly Update for 10/20/2019

Sunday, October 20, 2019

Greetings!

 

We are approaching the apex of the semester!   Family Conferences this week are the final school-wide step in fine-tuning our focus for the year.  Thanks, in advance, for arriving at your conference early and giving some forethought to the topics and questions you would like to discuss when we meet.  Please note that Middle Grades and Upper School students are REQUIRED to attend their conferences.  

 

Our soccer season will wrap up this week with semi-finals and finals playoffs.  Meanwhile, the conclusion of our Cross Country season has been delayed by a couple of weeks due to a combination of weather and school breaks.  Stay tuned for the date of the final meet.

 

NHS message from Kevin Cui:

Enjoy fantastic food while helping your community! Camelot NHS is joining with Zenfish, a restaurant in downtown Durham, for a fundraiser to support the NHS budget.  Our NHS community will receive 10% of the profit from any food Camelot families order!  Here is the address of the restaurant: 810 9th Street, Durham, NC, 27705. Please notify the restaurant that you are Camelot family members!  The project will be valid throughout the school year!  We sincerely appreciate your support!

I look forward to seeing many of you in conference this week!
