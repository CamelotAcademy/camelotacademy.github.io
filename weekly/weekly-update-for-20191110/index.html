Weekly Update for 11/10/2019

Sunday, November 10, 2019

Greetings!

 

November is flying by!  We are less than two weeks away from the Academic Fair and Fall Break. Our students and teachers are hard at work editing, revising, working on poster boards and polishing elevator speeches.  Meanwhile, the team worked together on every front to meet all deadlines, including the publishing of Mid Semester grades.  At our Thursday faculty meeting we took time to appreciate each other and the support and care we provide not just for our students, but also for each other.

 

Reminders:

 

School Picture Proofs were sent home – the order information is on the proof  - each student has a unique personal code to enter in when you go to the Strawbridge website – https://www.strawbridge.net/order-online/.  Note:  we do not have a master list of individual codes – that information would come directly from Strawbridge Studio.    Customer Services Phone: (866) 624-62

 

No pool this week - swimming class will meet but the pool is closed.

 

Bird watching walk with Mr. Thomas is Saturday, November 16 at 8 AM.  Please sign up here:  https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10c084ca4a622a1fa7-november
