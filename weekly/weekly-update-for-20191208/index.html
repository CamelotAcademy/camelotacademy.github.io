Weekly Update for 12/08/2019

Sunday, December 08, 2019

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to Senior Wyatt Pell on her acceptance to UNC-Wilmington!

Kudos to the Coyotes FLL Team who have qualified to advance to the NC State FLL Tournament in January!

 

The first week of December is already history and we are whizzing to the end of 2019.  This “Interlude” period between the two holiday breaks can be deceptive - short on calendar days but big in activity.  Please work with us to keep student routines and good habits in place, as that goes a long way towards making these days (and most days!) happy ones.

 

Announcing an exciting opportunity to help create programming!

Parents and students are invited to submit summer program proposals - with or without an attached instructor. We are seeking day, week, and multi-week ideas to implement for all ages - including multigenerational!  We are excited to explore the possibilities!  Deadline is Monday, December 16.

Proposal link is here:    https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2019/Camelot_Academy_Summer_Camp_Proposal.pdf

 

Camelot Families! 

 -Due to an issue with our opponent’s gym rental, we now have another Basketball HOME GAME!  This Friday, December 13th!  Because of this, we need two people to volunteer for the game; one person to help run the scoreboard and another person to keep the stats in a scorebook.  Please click the link below to sign up.  Thanks to all who have already graciously volunteered and signed up!!

https://www.signupgenius.com/go/5080F45A5AC2BA6FF2-basketball

 

Seeking a USED Mac device for use in GarageBand class – tablet or laptop.  Please let me know if you have one to donate.

 

Thank you to everyone who sent in recipes for the Camelot Family Cookbook. If you did not get the chance to send in your recipes yet, don’t worry, we are still collecting! The NHS is working to create a Camelot Family Cookbook that will be released next year, but we need your help. Please send in your recipes to camelotnhs@gmail.com or amy@camelotacademy.org. We will be accepting submissions all school year. We especially hope to see some great family recipes as we enter the holiday season. ️️️

Thank you for contributing,

National Honors Society

 

Reminder – Weather Alerts!:  In the event of weather that could impact travel safety, we will send an email and announce any changes to our operating schedule on WRAL and WTVD.  Since conditions vary widely across the geographical range of our community, please ALWAYS USE YOUR OWN JUDGEMENT as to road conditions in your area.
