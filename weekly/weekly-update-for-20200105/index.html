Weekly Update for 01/05/2020

Sunday, January 01, 2020

Greetings and Happy New Year!

 

I hope you and yours have had a wonderful Winter Break.  The staff and I greatly appreciate the outpouring of gifts, cards and yummy delights that we received as holiday gifts.  Many thanks for your thoughtfulness and generosity.

 

Also, many thanks to the parents who presented during our Holidays Around the World Morning Meetings:  Jessica and Patrick Harrell, Suman Gopinath, Manpreet & Manjeet Bhutani and Valerie Conklin.

 

The Class of 2020 has entered “their year” and they give us much to celebrate in terms of college acceptances.

Congrats to:

Robert Sutcliffe - UNC Asheville

Mack Taylor - Queens University (Charlotte) with a 46K Scholarship, University of Montana with a 24K Service Scholarship

Izzi Brown - University of San Francisco with a 100K Scholarship

Wyatt Pell - the Honors College at UNC Wilmington with a 30K Scholarship

 

Other Good News:

Our Cross Country team was honored for their winning season by the Mayor at the December 16th Durham City Council meeting.

The staff and faculty returned to school on Friday and put in a full day previewing all that is to come in this school year and Summer 2020.  Please pay careful attention to the details in this and future Weekly Updates as final exams, standardized testing, X Schedule and Family Conferences are all fast approaching and have some new twists.  In particular, please note that standardized testing is in FEBRUARY during Family Conferences (Feb 5-7).  Only students in the state-required grades of 3, 6 and 9 will attend school those days (mornings only). Optional Care is available to others.  More details to follow.

 

Exam Review Sheets will go home with all Middle and Upper School students on Monday and Tuesday of this week.  All Math, Spanish, Latin, Mandarin and ELL classes will have a cumulative semester exam next week.  Parents should read, sign and return the Review Sheets.
