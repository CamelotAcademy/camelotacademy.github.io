Weekly Update for 01/26/2020

Sunday, January 26, 2020

Greetings!  It was a “short” but very full week at Camelot!

 

Congratulations to the following seniors for their college acceptances:

-Wyatt Pell on her acceptance to UNC Chapel Hill

- Heather Hartman on her acceptance to NC State

 

Our Middle and Upper School students enjoyed two days of career guest speakers that sparked a lot of interesting thought and dialogue. Many thanks to parents Lydia Wong, Jonathan Holt, Kristin Jones, Jeff Powrie, Jenny Campbell, Bill Hall, Bennett Kirkpatrick, Karen Saxer, Greg Stocking, David Kong, Jeremy Ramont., Heather Vahdat, Lisa Lanier, Ami Goldstein, Eric Olson and Jon Poehlman.

 

Our Robotics, Swim and Basketball teams all competed - and did very well.  The Basketball Knights now move on to tournament games.

 

Our X Schedule launched!  We journeyed to the farm, to lectures at UNC and to the Tuba Museum. We made homemade pork buns to celebrate the Chinese New Year, and we drove to the top of the Durham Landfill.  This list is just a fraction of the activity these four days – all with the help of a new 15 passenger van that has been added to our fleet!

 

On Monday the Civics students depart for a four-day trip to Washington, DC.  Those students should pay careful attention to the information and packing list sent home.

 

Speaking of travel, the Middle Grades students need to reserve their slots on the April Astrocamp trip by Monday. Please see the email that went out from Ms. Michele this week for details.

 

The coming week is Club Preview Week.  Click here (https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2020/2020_Spring_Club_Signup.pdf) for the full slate of offerings this semester.  Students should attend clubs of interest this week before signing up for the semester by Friday.  Camelot Community members of all ages are invited to audition for Mr. Scott’s play this week.  Details here: (https://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/).
