Weekly Update for 02/09/2020

Sunday, February 09, 2020

Greetings!

 

Congratulations…

-         To senior Heather Hartman on her acceptance to Embry-Riddle University

-         To senior Elliott Webster on his acceptance to UNC Asheville

-         To senior Zach Ireland on his acceptances to UNC Asheville and University of Colorado: Colorado Springs

-         To our Battle of the Books Team for their WIN at the competition vs Rogers Herr

-         To the FTC Robotics Team that competed last weekend at the regional qualifier in Asheville where they won the 2nd Place Inspire Award, the Third Place Think Award and the 1st Place Motivate and Alliance Finalist Awards

 

Kudos to Ms. Lori for coordinating our annual state-required standardized testing.  Her vision of a better way to administer the tests came to fruition this year with beautiful results.  Meanwhile, many thanks to Ms. Jill who once again coordinated a complicated conference schedule on behalf of us all.  Both Lori and Jill have a knack for not only pulling off these hugely challenging schedules, but also making it pleasant with treats and goodies along the way!  Well done, Ms. Judith and Ms. Leah who ran three great Optional Care days against the backdrop of difficult weather. Great job, Mr. Scott, for the very successful Open House yesterday.  Interest in our school is record-breaking and we appreciate everyone helping to spread the word.

 

THANK YOU to all of the parents and host parents who participated in Family Conferences last week.  February conferences are always invaluable and productive in aligning goals and processes to reach those goals.  Our Professional Team is deeply appreciative of the ongoing commitment of our families to work collaboratively.

 

THIS WEEK SECOND SEMESTER CLUBS LAUNCH!

Tomorrow we roll our Strategy Games Club and LEGO club.  New offerings at the farm and with our theater production also launch.  Don’t forget about SEWING club on Fridays!  WE NEED OUR ROSTERS FINALIZED.  Please click here for the form to sign up: https://www.camelotacademy.org/media/documents/2020/2020_Spring_Club_Signup.pdf

 

IF YOU DID NOT HAVE A CONFERENCE - Ms. Jill sent you the E Period Elective Form that needs to be returned with a parent signature.  Please return this by Monday Morning so your student can participate in classes.

 

FRIDAY is Valentine’s DAY and our NHS Students have set up a fun night for our younger students to allow parents a night off.

Parents Night Out / Science Night, sponsored by the National Honor Society, will be taking place on Friday, February 14th from 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Participants will rotate through different science activities and will be provided pizza dinner while parents enjoy their Valentine's Day. Please sign up your Grade K-5 child via the Sign-Up Genius link provided below. The $15 admission fee includes dinner, and can be paid by cash or check at the door.

https://www.signupgenius.com/go/70A0849A4A72DA6F94-parents

 

The Global Studies Certificate Program is now accepting applications from students who are interested in joining us in the next school year (2020-2021). This program is for students entering 10th and 11th grade next year who want to engage in global issues and develop skills that are essential for global citizenship. The application period closes on Tuesday, February 18th.  All applicants will have a personal interview on Friday, February 28th.  Information and applications are available at camelotacademy.org/global-studies.  Questions can be directed to Ms. Charlotte at charlotte@camelotacademy.org.

 

We are thrilled to announce that we have scheduled a Bird Walk at the Camelot Farm with Mr. Thomas leading us all in observations and discoveries.

Mark your calendar for Saturday, March 14 (rain date Sunday, March 15) and look for a Sign-Up Genius from Ms. Wendy this week.
