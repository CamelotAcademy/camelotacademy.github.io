Message from Seniors Mack Taylor and William Harrison:

Volunteer Opportunity - Water Station at the Florence Forth Race - March 7 - 7:10-9:30 AM:

Hello everyone! On March 7, the annual Florence Forth race in Durham is taking place. Florence Forth is a race that raises money to fund research for a brain disease called Autoimmune Encephalitis and to raise awareness of it.  The race has been held each year to commemorate Florence McDow, a Durham girl who died at the age of six because of the disease.  Camelot students will be running a water and cheer station by passing out water and Gatorade to the runners/walkers. The race starts at 8 AM, but volunteers need to set up starting at 7:10 AM.  The water station will be set up on the corner of Main St. and Morning Glory Road in Durham. This is a fun bonding experience for Camelot students and a great way for students to earn service hours for the school service hour count. All ages are welcome to participate, but students under the age of 12 will need an adult to accompany them. Sign up here:  https://www.signupgenius.com/go/70A054DA4AE2EA1F49-florence
