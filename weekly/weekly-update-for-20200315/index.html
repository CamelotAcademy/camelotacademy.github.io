Weekly Update for 03/15/2020

Sunday, March 15, 2020

Greetings!

 

I hope your weekend is providing the opportunity for your family to pause and regroup as we all set up for a “new normal” during the coming weeks.  We know many parents have been asked to work from home and others do not have that option - both scenarios present circumstances that need to be figured out alongside school routines/expectations.  We are looking at the next four days (Monday-Thursday) before our Faculty Retreat and Spring Break as an important time to gather info about best practices using the virtual format.  We know the same will be true on the home-side. My guess is patience, good humor and communication will be key! The teachers and I have a great deal of excitement about the potential of this exercise and are hard at work setting up plans for each of our students.  We will use our Friday planning day to take  feedback from all to fine-tune the process for post-Spring Break implementation.

 

As per my Friday email, Middle and Upper School students should expect to login to Google Classroom/Meet each day this week at the regular class meeting time.  The ONLY exception to this is that ELL, Latin, Linguistics and Spanish classes will not meet on Monday, they will begin on Tuesday. “Study hall” classes will not meet in a virtual format. Meetings will not necessarily be for the entire class period, but these first four days will be important to setting expectations and routines for the duration.

IMPORTANT FOR ALL!

-         We welcome parents to sign in and participate/ask questions…..most especially during these opening days.

-         Virtual attendance will be taken each day.  If your student is ill or has a conflict that does not allow participation, please email the office to notify us.

-         If you have trouble connecting or have class-specific questions, please EMAIL THE TEACHER AND COPY ME. (All teachers have an email address that is their firstname@camelotacademy.org).

-         Our office will have limited hours this week to handle routine administrative tasks and to process Mid Semester grades.  Please email or call before coming to the building to ensure that someone is available if you need to take care of something in person.

-         Online instruction will be Monday-Thursday (March 16-19) of this week.

-         Friday, March 20 there will be no instruction – Faculty Retreat

-         Monday-Friday, March 23-27 – SPRING BREAK – No online instruction

-         Monday, March 30 – Instruction resumes (I’ll send a Sunday Update on March 29 with additional info.)

 

Our staff is working very hard to put together worthwhile lessons to engage our students and to keep their academic progress on track.  Even more importantly, we know that for many of our students keeping some “normalcy” and contact during these unusual times is important to their well-being. We count ourselves as very fortunate for many reasons - most especially including our relationships with the families we serve.  I’ll be in touch as our plans roll out.  In the meantime, don’t hesitate to reach out.  Please take good care of yourselves. That is Priority Number One.  I send you all a virtual hug.

 

Best,

 

Thelma
