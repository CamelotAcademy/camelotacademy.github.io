VIRTUAL POLICIES AND UPDATES:

 

Posted on March 16:

Student participation in our virtual classrooms is subject to the same rules as our school-building classrooms.  All participation (written and verbal) is to be on topic and appropriate.  A first violation will result in a warning to parents and School Director. A second violation will result in your muted participation in group exchanges and a possible reduction in Participation Points.

 

Posted today (March 29):

Student usernames, handles, presentation both on camera and in general should be appropriate to our school community. As we navigate new arenas, a teacher may ask a student to modify his/her virtual presence and/or may suspend that student’s participation in a live group until a satisfactory resolution is achieved.

Attendance is taken in each class, each day and turned in for official record keeping.  Each teacher will post a class-specific Participation Policy this week that will include the details of satisfactory “attendance”  which may include - signing on and engaging in a live-time platform, submission of homework by a specified deadline, other pre-arranged criteria.  As with in-school attendance, parents are asked to email Ms. Jill (jill@camelotacademy.org) if they know their student will be absent for some or all classes on a given day.  Parents will be notified, as usual, if there are missing assignments.
