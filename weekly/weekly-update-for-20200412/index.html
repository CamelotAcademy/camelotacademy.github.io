Weekly Update for 04/12/2020

Sunday, April 12, 2020

Greetings!  Happy Easter and Passover to families celebrating these holidays!

 

I hope you are doing well. 

 

We are very proud of our students and all of their efforts to adjust to the “new normal.”  Tomorrow, I will have a short Zoom meeting with Middle and Upper School students at the end of C Period (around 11:40) to touch base about routines of attendance, homework, electives, etc.  If your Middle or Upper School student has not done so already, please encourage them to accept the invitation to the Morning Meeting Classroom that they received last week.  This platform allows us to send out daily reminders, directly to students.

 

In response to parent feedback, we are working to unify our method of noting homework assignments to create a “virtual planner” of sorts.  If you look at your student’s Google Classroom platform and click on the three lines on the top left you will see an option called To Do.  If you click there, you will find an aggregate of all assignments from all classes, in order of due date. (More specific assignment info is often found in the Google Classroom of each specific class.).  Our teachers are working to ensure assignments are recorded in a way that has them show up in the To Do list in the most efficient way possible.  Please take a look at this option and let us know if it is helpful in filling the gap of our trusty assignment notebooks, or if we should seek a different system for your student.

 

Along the lines of exploring your student’s online learning experience, we are offering three sessions this week to provide a Show and Tell (with Q&A) for parents interested in learning more about the Google Classroom platform and some of the other platforms most used by our teachers.  If you would like to join, please email the hosting teacher directly for an invite.

 

Monday at 2 PM:  irfan@camelotacademy.org

 

Tuesday at 7 PM:  thomas@camelotacademy.org

 

Thursday at 4 PM:  michelle@camelotacademy.org  (Specifically for parents of her students)

 

 

 

Also in response to parent feedback, we are offering “after school” optional study halls for Middle and Upper School students on Mondays and Wednesdays from 1:45-2:45 PM.  Advance sign up is needed and regular study hall fees apply.  Teachers may also recommend a student to these study halls, parents will be contacted to arrange. Students must participate “on camera” during this homework time.  Group size is limited to SIX per session.  If you would like to sign your student up for either day, please send me an email.

 

MAPP Parents - be sure to have your student “join” the Google Classroom for art, world language, music and PE classes!  Live classes are happening each week and many related resources have been posted.

 

Finally, on Saturday at 2 PM we will run a Virtual Open House for prospective families.  This is a new endeavor!  If you have friends interested in joining our Camelot Family, please have them register for this event through our website homepage,  Many thanks to Mr. Scott, Ms. Jill, Ms. Rita and Dain Anderson for their efforts on this front.

 

I hope you have a great week.

 

Best,

Thelma
