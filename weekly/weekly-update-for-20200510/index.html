Weekly Update for 05/10/2020

Sunday, May 10, 2020

Greetings and HAPPY MOTHER’S DAY to all the moms and folks who provide maternal love and care.  Thank you for being a constant source of inspiration to me and to us all.

 

Celebrate:

 

We launched a successful Virtual X Schedule for Middle and Upper School students this week.  Once again students, parents and teachers have worked together with exemplary teamwork to make it all happen.

 

Announce:

 

Speaking of “make it all happen” - virtual mode is no deterrent to our Ms. Jenny’s fun spirit.  She has persisted in finding a way to offer a school dance (Grades 7-12) and it is scheduled for this Friday night - May 15 from 7-9 PM!  Camelot is teaming up with the local radio show Pulse FM to host this virtual dance,  Schools from around the area will create a Zoom or Hangout where students can dance together to songs played on their station. Tomorrow, Pulse will be announcing more details for this event.  We will send out a link to the information.  Camelot will have a Zoom or Hangouts link where students can join their classmates.  They can dress up, share their camera, decorate their space, and enjoy!  Camelot will also be sponsoring prizes in several categories. We hope your student will plan to join us for this unique event!

 

Thanks to Ms. Judith, our students will also be getting school T-Shirts and yearbooks to commemorate this amazing year!  Stay tuned for an email later this week that will allow you to set up porch pick up or mailing.
