Weekly Update for 05/17/2020

Sunday, May 17, 2020

Greetings!

 

Welcome to the last week of the 2019-20 school year! Once again, this email is full of important info.  Thanks for your attention to it all.

 

Kudos to Ms. Jenny for a successful Virtual Dance and thanks to all the staff and students (and parents!) who joined in to help create a fun evening.  We hope to share the pictures at the Senior Yearbook Zoom Meeting on Thursday at 1 PM.

 

There was much JOY this week at the Class of 2020 speech rehearsal.  I was happy to find that the circumstances of the last few months have not overshadowed their graduation messages or their hopes and excitement for the future.  One senior’s speech happens to talk quite extensively about a quality that we have chosen for the 2020-21 Theme - RESILIENCE.  I can’t wait for you to hear directly from these young people when you listen to their remarks during the Graduation Livestream on Saturday night.

 

ANNOUNCE:                                                                                                                                                       

 

SUMMER PROGRAM -  REBOOT options are on our website, thanks to Ms. Rita and our creative teachers who were excited to take the feedback you provided in the parent survey and turn it into action. Programming begins on June 8 in virtual mode.  July and August options include virtual offerings, with some opportunities to pivot to onsite, if circumstances allow.  Be sure to check out both the tab for Edu-Ventures (ages 6-12) and Explorations (Multigenerational).   Summer School in both Language Arts and Math is also offered in an individualized format with the content and focus uniquely planned for each participant. We have a few once-a-week classes yet to add (Mindful Movement, etc.  Stay tuned!) You can check it all out here: https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/#/home

 

If you would be willing to help get the word out about our Summer Programs we would appreciate it.  Families who would like to display a yard sign (“Summer Done Differently”) from now through July 15th may earn Parent Service credit for the semester. We will leave one labeled with your name for pick up from the porch.  Please send Wendy an email (Wendy@camelotacademy)  if you would like a sign and also please be sure to share the program link with your friends!

 

School-issued books and supplies can be returned to the porch any time this week or next (but not during the parade, when everyone must stay in their cars). Please put the items in a bag, labeled with the student name, and place the bag on one of the tables on the SIDE PORCH.

 

Summer Reading information (for rising 3rd-12th graders) is being sent out on Monday.  We have added some live-time Q and A sessions for parents/teachers in the coming weeks.  Please note the specifics and we will send out additional reminders in the days to come.

 

The first day of the 2020-21 school year Is Wednesday, August 26.  Parent Orientation will be Monday, August 24 and, like this year, we will offer sessions throughout the day.  Much more about all of this will come home through the mail in a couple of weeks.

-Friday

SATURDAY, MAY 23 - GRADUATION!

 

-10:15 AM - All families of underclassmen meet in the parking lot of Lakewood Mall (near Scrap Exchange) to get in a caravan. They will then proceed in a line out the north entrance, right onto Morehead Ave to Shepherd Street to Camelot.

 

-10:15 The graduating seniors and their families will be parked in cars facing the lot, on the far side from the building.  The caravan will pull through slowly to wave and cheer the graduates, who will be standing beside their family cars in their caps and gowns.

 

-After proceeding through the parking lot, families of underclassmen are free to continue on with their plans for the day.  The seniors and their families will remain for the awarding of diplomas.

 

-We hope everyone will tune in for the Livestream of the full ceremony - including MAPP and other musical performances, senior speeches, awarding of diplomas, etc.  - at 6 PM (NOTE TIME CHANGE). The details of how to view the Livestream will be sent later next week.

 

 

…and BEYOND….

 

 

Our teachers will be working the week of May 25 to finish grading and report writing.  We look forward to debriefing on all we have learned from the virtual experience and sharing ideas for the many creative things we look forward to going forward.  Obviously, our planning will include many options to accommodate whatever COVID-related variables we find ourselves working with in mid to late summer.  I can tell you that we have been cataloging our ideas throughout these weeks and there are many professional insights that will enhance our repertoire for all times to come.  The opportunity to have the summer to fully engage with these ideas is exciting.  We will be reaching back out to you over the coming weeks to collect more of your input on everything from course offerings to scheduling. In the meantime, keep those emails to me coming - I appreciate each and every perspective I receive.

 

 

 

It has been a distinct honor to work with you and your children through this historic time.  Between the support that was shown me, personally, in the fall when my mother passed away and the collegiality I’ve shared with you in virtual mode, it is fair to say that there has never been a Camelot Family with whom I have felt more connected than you, the members of 2019-20. Please join me in making this a wonderful last week of the school year and a special time for our Class of 2020 and their families.

 

 

I will follow up next week with reminders and other housekeeping items.  In the meantime, I hope your Memorial Day weekend will be the beginning of a healthy and happy summer.

 

 

Best,

Thelma
