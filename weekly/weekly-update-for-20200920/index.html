Weekly Update for 09/20/2020

Sunday, September 20, 2020

Greetings!

 

The weather is setting a true “Back to School” feel and there is much excitement for the coming days that will start to bring us together on site. Although much about our new routines are challenging - on both the school and home front - I find myself filled with HOPE that we are figuring it out, moving forward and TOGETHER we will build an outstanding experience for our young people.

 

As folks of all grade levels are aware, the start of Module 2 tomorrow brings post-Retreat schedule changes.  (See my Sept. 18 email for background.)

-         MAPP families have received an email (Friday) from the classroom teachers with that info

-         Middle Grades families received and email from Ms. Michelle (Friday) with info as to where students can access their individual schedules

-         Upper School students have info about Module 2 in the Google Classroom of their B Rotation class.

 

Please remember to use the HOTLINE for any questions connecting with classes (919-886-5792)

 

Reminders for those coming to campus/farm this week:

-         ONLY come if your student and everyone in your household is free from all COVID-related symptoms

-         Bring inhalers and epipens, if applicable

-         Pre-apply or bring bug spray or sunscreen, if so desired

-         Bring a water bottle

-         Bring all instructor-requested supplies

-         Arrive and pick up on time (Please double check your class schedule with your instructor)

-         Parents/drivers, please STAY IN YOUR CAR - pull down the lot and wait in the car line for screening and instructions

 

 

SUPPLY PICK UP - Many grade levels!

 

Several instructors have left items, including texts, for pick up over the weekend:  JR MAPP, Ms. Jenny (math), Ms. Erin (Latin) and Ms. Charlotte (Spanish).

You should have received an email from your instructor if this applies to you.

 

 

UPPER SCHOOL

-         English instruction this module is being conducted via ONE-ON-ONE weekly conferences with teachers about Academic Fair progress. 

-         Each student should go to their Google Classroom for English to sign up for a meeting time.  The sign-up automatically generates a calendar entry and link.

-         8 AM Monday (tomorrow) Academic Fair research deadline for all Upper School students.  Please meet this deadline!

 

 

The Week Ahead:

 

Monday:

Begin Module 2

 

Thursday:

Virtual Parent Night (a required parent event). 

At Parent Night you will meet with each of your student’s classroom teachers to learn about specific class curriculum, routines, materials and expectations. (MAPP @ 5 PM, Middle Grades @ 6 PM, Upper School @ 7 PM).

(UPPER SCHOOL PARENTS - please have your student’s login info for Google Classroom (preferred) or a list of your student’s classes and teachers - to facilitate your experience in meeting the right teachers in virtual mode)

 

 

UPCOMING:

 

October 5-16 – Module 3 (Upper School C Class Rotation)

 

Week of October 19 each Camelot Family will have a Zoom conference with the full teaching team for their students.  At that time we will review Pretest Results and early progress.  We will also use that opportunity to collaborate with you on any fine-tuning needed to the plans

 

 

I hope you have a great week.

 

Best,

 

Thelma
