Weekly Update for 09/27/2020

Sunday, September 27, 2020

Greetings and special well wishes for all those celebrating Yom Kippur.

Camelot enjoyed another momentous week both by welcoming Middle and Upper School students to campus and by meeting with all of our parents at Parent Night.  It feels like the purposeful efforts being made by all to maintain strong lines of communication are working well.

The week also brought many heartfelt moments that I expect to always remember.  Witnessing “screen friends” meet each other in person for the first time and noting the overwhelming spirit of cooperation in our students of all ages literally brought tears to my eyes on more than one occasion. Our dedicated teaching team not only worked very long hours on Thursday night, but many were onsite early Friday morning to help with set-up and drop-off routines during our first heavy rains of the on-campus season. It’s powerful and humbling to witness such dedication.
