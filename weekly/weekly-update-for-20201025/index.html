Weekly Update for 10/25/2020

Sunday, October 25, 2020

Greetings, Camelot Families!

 

Thank you for a productive round of conferences. We found the exchanges to be especially worthwhile and inspiring, and hope you did too. I will be using your ideas and input to inform the Quarter Two Survey coming to you later this week.

 

In addition to conferences, last week we also had some major excavation work done on the playground and lower yard.  You will note a substantive trench that awaits the next stage of work replacing the 100+ year old drainage system.  We are excited about the new garden and playground enhancements that this will allow.  It will be a few weeks before the next stage of work occurs.

 

Tomorrow we launch Module 4 - the last module of the First Quarter. For Upper School students, this is a D Rotation.  D class assignments were reviewed in conference and I sent an email yesterday to Upper School families with info as to how to connect to classes.

Please be reminded that ALL classes are VIRTUAL on Mondays.

 

EIGHT NEW FACULTY MEMBERS ARE FEATURED THIS MODULE!  It is a super big week - Ms. Jillian and Ms. Diane are teaching art and assisting with MAPP students , Ms. Raleigh will be working with all-virtual Upper School AM art students, while Ms. Robin works with all on-site Upper School AM art students. In addition, Ms. Erin (Sullivan) will be teaching theater to Upper School while Ms. Sasha brings Music to the Middle Grades.  We also welcome the return of Megan Fitz (Upper School afternoon art) and Patricia Johnson (math asst.) to the mix.  While we get this team settled in the “COVID Normal,” please have extra patience - especially with parking lot routines.
