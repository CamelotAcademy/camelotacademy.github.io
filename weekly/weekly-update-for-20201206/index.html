ANNOUNCEMENTS:

 

Two announcements from the National Honor Society:

 

1)     December Service Saturday

 

This December, the Camelot National Honor Society is continuing our Service Saturdays initiative. Thank you to everyone who picked up trash last month. This month, we are encouraging members of the Camelot community to donate a toy to the Toys for Tots program. For those unfamiliar with the great work of Toys for Tots, according to its website, during the holiday season, “Coordinators, with the assistance of local social welfare agencies, church groups, and other local community agencies, distribute the toys to the less fortunate children of the community.” This year more than ever, it would be amazing to brighten the holiday season for less fortunate children. Due to the pandemic, Toys for Tots is receiving fewer toys than in previous years. At the link below, you have the option of picking out a toy and dropping it in a virtual toy box to purchase - contact free! The NHS hopes you have a happy holiday season!

 

https://toysfortotsbox.com/?utm_source=ToysforTotsFoundation&utm_medium=Website_DonateAToy&utm_campaign=ToysforTots_from_TFT
