Weekly Update for 01/03/2021

Sunday, January 03, 2021

Greetings and Happy New Year!

 

I hope your family has had a pleasant break.   We have much to look forward to in the coming weeks and months - not the least of which is the impending birth of two faculty baby boys sometime in the next month!  Mr. Krista/Mr. Dan and Mr. Ab/Erika all have the same due date (really - what are the odds?). 2020 gave us much practice in scheduling gymnastics - bring it on!

 

Congratulations to Senior Emily Ren on her acceptance to New York University.

 

Announcements:

Thanks to Ms. Leah for the great job designing the school holiday card. We were pleased to be able to maintain the tradition of sending a whole school greeting.

 

The Camelot playground has been the site of much work over the past few weeks.  Although it has more enhancements to come, it will be ready for children next week.

 

The Yearbook Club is running a Cover Art contest.

Here are the requirements for submissions:

·       Cover must reflect this year’s theme of “Resilience”

·       Must be drawn on 8 ½ x 11 standard sized paper

·       Can’t be drawn on lined paper, but can be free hand drawings

·       Camelot Academy somewhere in large bold lettering

·       Don’t forget to include 2020-2021, and to put your name on back so we can give credit.

·       Photography entries are welcomed!

·       Deadline for rough drafts is January 22nd

All Classes at all grade levels are virtual this week.

Take good care.  I hope your year gets off to a great start.
