Yearbook Announcement:

The Yearbook Team is doing at-home school portraits and needs an individual portrait picture of each student. When we receive the pictures, we well be editing out the background and replacing it with a uniform background so it will look like we were able to do in-person photos.

Portrait Requirements:

    - Should be able to see SHOULDERS TO HEAD with a little bit of clearance above the head.

   - Must be taken VERTICALLY

   - Solid background

   - Try and take it in natural lighting

   - Look presentable

   - *If you have a phone that has portrait mode DO NOT use it*

   - Include the students name in the title of the photo when it is submitted

Global Studies Announcement:

 

Camelot’s Global Studies Certificate Program is now accepting applications for the 2021-2022 school year!

 

  This is a 2-year program for students who are committed to engaging in their communities and the world.

  Rising 10th and 11th grade students may apply, but students of any age may attend our interest meeting to learn more.

  After completing the program, students will receive a certificate and notation of their transcript in recognition of their commitment to Global Citizenship.

 

  Interested students are invited to join us after school on Tuesday, February 2 at 3:45 PM to ask questions and hear from current program participants. (Meeting link will be posted closer to the date)

 

Application deadline is February 16, 2021.
