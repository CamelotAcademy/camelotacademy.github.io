ANNOUNCE:

Message from NHS students Natalie Bailey and Elizabeth Whetzel:

 

In times like these, it is important that we come together (figuratively) and continue to support our community. For many years, the Camelot Family has been a big supporter of Empty Bowls. Empty Bowls brings together local chefs, with the help of Urban Ministries, to feed and raise money for the hungry and homeless in Durham. Though we sadly can’t help the project in person this year, there are many new ways that we get to show our support. This year Camelot will be an official sponsor for Empty Bowls and you can help too! To get involved, you can help out our neighbors in need simply by digging into some delicious soup! In buying a ticket, you can get an individual meal-sized bowl to pick up on March 4 from a participating restaurant of your choice. All you need to do is drive by your chosen restaurant during their pick-up time, and enjoy.

For more information go to:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/empty-bowls-2021-supporting-umd-presented-by-the-forest-at-duke-tickets-139790861229?blm_aid=52230
