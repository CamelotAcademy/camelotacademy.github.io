The story “behind the scenes” as to how Camelot Report Cards are produced is one of time and dedication of the whole team.  In addition to the grading and recording tasks that are key to our mastery-based approach, our teachers spend many hours writing comments that document and explain the work and growth of the semester.  Once those grades and comments are collected, all info needs to be cross-checked and verified, templates are locked and made into PDF’s  - mostly by Donna, our data consultant (and my sister).  While she is doing that data verification, I am reading and reviewing each comment for each class for each student - upwards of 500 individually-crafted entries.  All of this info then flows to Ms. Wendy for printing and first round collating (by subject).  Ms. Jill takes over with the coordination at the student-level and cross-checks, yet again, that there are no missing pieces.  Meanwhile, Donna and I are also making sure this data is set up to flow to high school transcripts, which requires yet another level of validating - mostly of notating our very individualized math system in a format that works for colleges. So.......when the portal opens on Tuesday and the postal worker carts away the envelopes with all the hard copies heading to your homes, please set aside a time to read and reflect on the information we share - with much Love - your Camelot Valentine!  As we approach Family Conferences in mid-March**, this info will provide an important backdrop to our exchanges.
