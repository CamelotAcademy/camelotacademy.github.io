Weekly Update for 03/14/2021

Sunday, March 14, 2021

Greetings and Happy Daylight Savings Time!

 

We had a lovely sun-filled week that allowed the Environmental Engineering class to make good progress on the bioswale they are installing behind the playground.  The weather also permitted MAPP students to eat their first lunch on campus in picnic fashion. Meanwhile, the Middle Grades Theater classes celebrated the end of the module with puppet shows entirely of their own design - from scripts to sock puppets.

 

Behind the scenes: the faculty is especially busy with an array of tasks. Yesterday they hosted a very well attended open house. In addition, they are prepping for conferences, finishing the work of the third quarter in preparation for mid-semester reporting and gearing up for expanded on-campus routines in Q4.

 

Each week in our full faculty meeting we take a few moments to share examples of how we are practicing the three principles on which our school is based:

- Individualized

- Mastery based

- Parents as Team members

If your family is scheduled for a conference this week, we look forward to discussing how these values are translating to your student’s experience. Please take a few moments before your meeting to reflect and to prepare any comments or questions you may have on first semester reports and progress or any other items.
