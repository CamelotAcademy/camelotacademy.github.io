Weekly Update for 03/21/2021

Sunday, March 21, 2021

Greetings and Happy Spring!

 

Congratulations to the following seniors on these recent college acceptances:

LM Fountain - Auburn University

Sydney Kamin - American University

Sylvia Xu - University of California - Davis

 

Thank you, parents and families, for the great turnout last week at the Resilience presentations of our AP English students.  Also, thanks for a productive and affirming round of family conferences.  Our teaching team came away from the meetings with a strong sense of mutual appreciation and cooperation in the important work we do together.

 

 

Behind the Scenes:

The Camelot Farm has been bustling with activity.  The historic cabin has had its second story installed.  Josh Smith has been prepping and doing some early planting of the hugel beds.  The five chicks that surprised us by their December hatching moved out with the “big girls” this week. To celebrate, all the hens have resumed laying and we will soon have students take over the “egg business” again. Also, we are preparing for the arrival of two hives of honeybees in a couple of weeks. Middle Grades students are scheduled to join the MAPP classes to check it all out before Spring Break.
