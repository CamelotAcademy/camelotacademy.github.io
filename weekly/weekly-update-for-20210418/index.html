Weekly Update for 04/18/2021

Sunday, April 18, 2021

Greetings!

 

Many thanks to all the families who came to campus yesterday for Garden Day!  Thanks to the vision of Mr. Thomas, and the donation of plants and time by so many, we have in the works a poetically beautiful testimony to the power of community.  Much appreciation to Mr. Josh , Mr. Tom, Tyler and Claire for ensuring that everything went smoothly and safely.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

 

It was a banner week on campus with many times where every bit of indoor and outdoor space was bustling with activity.  Middle Grades students launched their bottle rockets at the farm, MAPP students were hard at work preparing for their Academic Fair presentations and Upper School AP English students wrote practice essays to the backdrop of Evening of the Arts performance rehearsals.  Special thanks to Ms. Leah who bravely and expertly helped wrangle and oversee all the new “happenings”!
