Sunday, April 25, 2021

Greetings!

 

Happy Wedding Day to our Ms. Anna and her partner Sam!

 

Congratulations to Senior Sydney Kamin on her acceptance to Sarah Lawrence College.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Ms. Wendy is FINALLY getting to again use her vast expertise in event planning for our many upcoming celebrations!  Of course, everything is re-imagined - there will be multiple smaller events, all to be held at outdoor venues we haven’t used before, while working with layers of COVID protocols unique to each setting.  After months of Ms. Wendy holding down the fort in a too-quiet building, there is no doubt - WE ARE BACK!  Much appreciation to her for helping us create these special moments for our students and their families.
