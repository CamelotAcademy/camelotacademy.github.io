Weekly Update for 05/16/2021

Sunday, May 16, 2021

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to senior Sofy Fonseca on her acceptance to the Maryland Institute College of Art.

 

Thanks to the MAPP parents and teachers for helping us execute reimagined versions of the MAPP Academic Fair and performances at the farm.  It was a true joy to gather together and celebrate the work and talent of our youngest students.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Shortages in gas availability added some interest to the week. We are grateful to have the ability to pivot on a dime to our virtual platforms.  We won’t complain, however, if the last module of the year provides no further need for such! Thank you to all for your ongoing flexibility and support.

 

 

REMIND:

SUMMER CAMP AND SUMMER SCHOOL info and registration is here:

https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/#/home

 

All of our COVID protocols remain in place these last two weeks of school. Your ongoing support and communication is deeply appreciated by every staff member. We look forward to simpler times ahead!

 

Middle Grades parents should have received and acknowledged Exam Review Sheets from math teachers.  Upper School parents should have received and acknowledged Exam Review Sheets from math, Spanish and Mandarin teachers.

 

ANNOUNCE:

Our final week of school - and details regarding ERB testing, Middle Grades plans and graduation continue to evolve and reflect incoming variables.  Please pay particular attention to bolded (updated) info below.

 

All parents of students taking (virtual)6th, (all)9th, (some)11th or other special circumstance grade level testing (State scholarship students) will receive an email on Monday from Ms. Wendy with more testing details and a link to a required parent Zoom meeting this Thursday (May 20) at 7 PM.
