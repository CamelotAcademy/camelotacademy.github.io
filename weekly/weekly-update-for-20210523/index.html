Weekly Update for 05/23/2021

Sunday, May 23, 2021

Greetings!

 

We have arrived at the final week of the school year!!!  Thanks for your support and collaboration throughout this remarkable journey - as one or our teachers commented this week, “In all of my years of teaching, I’ve never felt more connected to my students’ parents.”

 

There are many important details for your attention this week.  Clearly, with so much “business” and fun to fit in we will all need to keep this info handy as a roadmap - each day, maybe even each hour!

 

Congratulations to the newly elected slate of NHS offices for the 2021-22 school year:

 

President - Natalie Bailey

Vice President of Projects - Anna Bailey

Vice President of Communications - Eli Terry

Treasurer - Nelle Poehlman

Secretary - Thomas Smith

 

 

REMIND:

Camelot is offering both Summer School and Summer Camp at the farm.  Check out all the info here:

https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/#/home

 

ANNOUNCE:

Books and materials need to be returned to school this week.  Collection boxes are on the porch. 

 

Students rising to grades 3-12 have Summer Reading assignments.  SR MAPP, Middle Grades and Upper School each have their own lists and plans.  Middle Grades and Upper School info has been released.  SR MAPP will be released by tomorrow.

 

Ms. Michelle is offering day-long hiking trips to various destinations for rising 5th-12th graders - and a one night trip - the week of June 7.  Please click here for more information: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1w5iyw_xnugvmIKbuCe1txHZzma2Sd7zfYr1rsSFZ8Qk/edit?usp=sharing
