Weekly Update for 06/08/2021

Sunday, June 08, 2021

Greetings!

 

I hope your summer is off to a great start.

 

We had teacher work days through last Friday and now we turn to the large task of producing end of year grades, comments and reports. Perhaps the highlight of last week was a faculty gathering on the porch.  It was delightful to be together in person and to once again share summer plans that include travel by our staff to many destinations - including England and Alaska! Our gathering also included a heartfelt send-off to our treasured colleague Dr. Erin Grady as she prepares to embark on the next step in her professional journey, as a teacher at Ravenscroft School in Raleigh.

 

I want to include one last round of thanks to the many staff members who made graduation weekend full of lovely memories for us all.

-         Greg, his partner Cami, Josh and Tyler for preparation and site management at the farm

-         Mr. Tom and Mr. Thomas for their assistance on site

-         Ms. Lori and team for orchestrating the parade

-         Ms. Robin and the yearbook team for the Whole School Memories slideshow - Link here: https://drive.google.com/file/d/19lGBj98MDJcqYHgDiEn8R-Rxdje3wxAu/view?usp=sharing

-         Ms. Amy, Ms. Lori and Mr. Dan for the Senior Send-off Movie (uncut version) link here:  https://youtu.be/xqmnDdsl-ms

-         Ms. Wendy for orchestrating details from flowers to diplomas - and so much more

-         Mr. Irfan - for his genius in editing all the pieces together into one smooth and lovely production - from parade to hats off!  A keepsake video can be ordered here:    https://forms.gle/Kjnc5iK6M46GfcLQA
