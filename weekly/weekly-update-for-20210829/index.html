Weekly Update for 08/29/2021

Sunday, August 29, 2021

Greetings!

 

We had a great opening and Orientation and I hope that your students came home from our first two days together excited about the new year. Huge thanks to Ms. Lori, Ms. Amy, Ms. Michelle, Mr. Ab, Mr. Tommy, Ms. Anna and Ms. Leah for orchestrating the myriad of student activities.  Kudos to the whole team who helped with set-up and execution - before, during and after!  “Way to go!” - Wendy, Jill and Donna on the myriad of emails, publications and communications. Thank you, thank you to the many families who donated chairs and furnishings for our outdoor Camelot Village and to the students who helped with the set-up!

 

On Monday, our routines switch to more of a class rhythm in our outdoor spaces.  As you may recall, we are conducting an extended period of REVIEW, so very little - if any - “pre-assessments” will take place this week.  We have allowed generous additional time for students (and teachers) to learn and practice routines and to learn their schedules.  We will literally be “calling” class changes from the microphone every hour - I anticipate it feeling a bit like a square dance!  I will continue to take time this week to work with individual students to adjust their schedules to best meet their needs and desires.

 

REMINDERS:

 

Today is the FINAL DEADLINE to OPT-IN to onsite COVID testing, scheduled to start one day next week. (Date TBD by the administering program - we will tell you as soon as we know.) Here is the link: https://home.color.com/covid/sign-up/start?partner=tf-cam-120-students

For those who choose NOT to opt in, we will give you the date next week by which you will need to take your student for COVID screening by a provider of your choice.  As you are aware, these screenings will be WEEKLY - for all students and staff on campus - while our community remains in a state of high metrics.

 

Effective tomorrow, Module 1 Arrival and Departure windows go into effect. 

 

JR and SR MAPP

- arrive 8:30-8:45 AM, depart 3:00-3:15 PM

 

Middle Grades

- arrive 8:15-8:30 AM, depart 2:45-3:00 PM

 

Upper School

- arrive 15 mins. before class start (8:30 or 9:30 or 10:30 AM)

- depart 15 mins. after class end (2:30 or 3:30 PM)

 

(NOTE: siblings and carpoolers arrive/depart on earliest/latest time that applies, with no before/after school fees)

 

Although Before and After School programming begins tomorrow (7:30 AM- 5:30 PM), we request that until the testing noted above is in place you use this program minimally, only as urgently needed.  This will allow us to best minimize the mixing of students across divisions.
