Weekly Update for 09/05/2021

Sunday, September 05, 2021

Greetings and Happy September!

 

I hope this email finds you enjoying the weather on this long holiday weekend.  Our outdoor classes were “the place to be” on  Thursday and Friday and we are looking forward to more beautiful days!

 

 

REMIND:

 

School is closed tomorrow (Monday, September 6), in observance of Labor Day.

 

We appreciate your continued minimal use of before and after school services during this pre-COVID screening week.  The teamwork on this has been phenomenal.  The teamwork has also been outstanding in keeping students who are ill at home and keeping in close communication with me throughout. 

We especially appreciate the efforts to have students stay home if ANYONE in the household has symptoms.  

 

To date, we have no known COVID exposures on campus!

 

Updates to the Parent Portal have been posted and include:

-         2021-22 Family Handbook

-         Opening 2021 COVID Protocols

-         Student COVID vaccination status info

Please download and answer corresponding items on Questions Tab to indicate you have reviewed. 

(Detailed instructions can be found in the Update from last week - August 29, 2021.)

 

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

Welcome to the Wilson family - Alia has joined Jr MAPP and Evelyn has joined Middle Grades.

 

Athletic forms have been posted on our website here: https://www.camelotacademy.org/parents/

Interested 5th-12th grade students may sign up for after school soccer or cross country practices beginning the week of September 13.

 

Our weekly on-site COVID screening is scheduled to begin this Friday (September 10) and will be conducted every Friday going forward.  (We have requested an earlier day this week, if possible, but most likely that will not occur.  If it becomes an option, we will let you know in advance.) Each family will receive test results via email or text within 24 hours. The school will also receive test results and will follow up with any families who receive positive results as to next steps.

Here is a link to FAQ about this testing service:

https://makostrongschoolsnc.com/faqs

 

As you are aware, we are keeping our classes outside until we have our first round of these testing metrics.  We will, of course, share that metric info with you as well as plans for next steps in our roll-out. We are grateful that each day has brought new enhancements to our Camelot Village set-up and excellent instruction is well underway.  We anticipate many classes choosing to take advantage of the outdoor set-up even after we start pivoting indoors - especially when we have this kind of weather as a backdrop!

 

 

I hope you have a great week.  To all those celebrating Rosh Hashana, we wish you a happy holiday!

 

Best,

Thelma
