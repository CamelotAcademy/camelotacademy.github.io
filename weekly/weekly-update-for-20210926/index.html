Weekly Update for 09/26/2021

Sunday, September 26, 2021

Greetings and Happy Fall!

 

We had a very productive faculty retreat and are excited to resume instruction with the benefit of your input and our reflection.  Our review of each student included things to watch, tweak and try in our work to maximize opportunities for nurturing talents and passions and addressing challenges.  Our goal of creating a joyful environment in which each community member can flourish is ongoing and constant.  As intended, there will be some fine-tuning of scheduling and classes resulting from our work, most of which will be communicated this week and implemented next week to coincide with the start of Module 3 on Monday, October 4. We will also address topics/answer feedback applicable to all when we meet with you via Zoom for Parent Night on Thursday, October 7. 

 

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

In addition to three days of meetings, both grounds work and interior work took place to prepare our building and grounds for the upcoming weeks.  Ms. Lori continues to have “visions” of new ways to use space - and is joined by many inspired colleagues to reimagine and reinvent our use of inside and outside spaces. 

 

 

REMIND:

Please display your student name car visors at pick-up time. This allows us to manage dismissal with maximum efficiency.

 

SOCKTOBER donations are appreciated. NHS has a collection box on the porch.

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

COVID surveillance testing this week will be both Monday afternoon (to replace last Friday) as well as this Friday morning.  Going forward, we will maintain the once a week Friday morning schedule.

 

Parking lot screening will be scaled back to every Monday and to random days - as needed, to remind/reinforce protocols - starting this week.  We are deeply appreciative of the vigilance of all in “pre-screening” at home and for your efforts to reach out to me with questions.

 

PARENT NIGHT ZOOM SCHEDULE    Thursday, October 7, 2021

JR and SR MAPP - 5:30 PM

Middle Grades - 6:30 PM

Upper School - 7:30 PM

 

Please note this is a REQUIRED parent event.  Our agenda includes covering all general info - for each class - so that when we convene for Family Conferences the week of October 18 we can spend our time solely on exchanging information about your student and not on whole-class protocols or expectations. 

 

 

I hope you have a great week.  Thank you, again, for your support of our work on behalf of your children.

 

Best,

 

Thelma
