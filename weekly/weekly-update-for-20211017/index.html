Weekly Update for 10/17/2021

Sunday, October 17, 2021

Greetings!

 

Thank you to our coaches and teams for an exciting week.  Coach Carlos and the soccer team and Coach Tom and the Cross Country Team participated in meet-ups with our peer schools that helped to create many fun memories of the season.  Special thanks to Athletic Director Michael Berro and Ms. Magda for their many contributions.  Shout-outs to the parent and student volunteers and fans!

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

           

The first big college deadline was this week and the Class of ‘22 is now out the gate!  Many more applications will follow by November 1.  It is always a joy to work with our seniors on their applications and to imagine the many exciting possibilities for their futures.

 

REMINDERS:

 

Family conferences are this week!  Each family has been scheduled to meet with the teaching team. 

-         No class for Middle and Upper School all week.

-         No class for MAPP students Wednesday through Friday

-         Please arrive to your Zoom meeting on time, with 5th-12th grade students in attendance, and with your thoughts and questions ready to share!

 

If you have not already, please fill out the Outdoor Education Expectations Parent Survey:  https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe8axX6OuW9YqY_ENiloPZd8MtmREDH9pZPNfr_tVbEjX1CvA/viewform?usp=sf_link

 

ANNOUNCEMENTS:

 

Optional Care is being offered Wednesday-Friday for MAPP and Middle Grades students. Sign up is here: https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10C084CA4A622A1FA7-2021

 

Our Friday COVID surveillance testing (October 15) again came back with 0% positive!  Since we do not have classes this Friday, we will have testing the afternoon of Monday, October 25 and – again – that Friday morning (October 29)!

 

I look forward to seeing many of you in conference.  I hope you have a great week.

 

Best,

Thelma
