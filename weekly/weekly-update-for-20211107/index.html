Weekly Update for 11/07/2021

Sunday, November 07, 2021

Greetings!

THERE IS A LOT OF IMPORTANT INFO HERE!

 

I hope you will enjoy the “extra” hour of weekend!

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

English teachers Ms. Amy, Ms. Michelle, Mr. Tom and Mr. Thomas are deep in the process of working with students to produce their Academic Fair papers and presentations.  Hours and hours and HOURS of editing, conferencing, brainstorming, and planning are ongoing.  We look forward to sharing the end results with you on Friday, November 19! (See below - UPCOMING - for the Where and How.)

 

REMIND:

Thanks to Ms. Rita, Ms. Jill and Donna De Carlo for their great work in getting Mid Semester grades and comments coordinated and released.  Please be reminded that these grades are NOT official or permanent - they are simply a communication between school and home as to where things stood as of Friday, October 29. 

 

If you received a Mid Semester Warning, please respond to the email with acknowledgement of receipt.

 

Candy drive to benefit the troops:

We have already gotten an astounding amount of candy, thank you for all of the donations! Next Friday will be the last day of the project, so we would greatly appreciate any final contributions!  Please drop your donations off at the blue tent.

 

Monday is PICTURE DAY!  Individual portraits will be taken - on the porch - by Strawbridge Studios. Proof packages for purchase will be sent home in a few weeks.  Photos taken will also be used for our yearbook.

 

ANNOUNCE:

We are excited to begin previews/info sessions of clubs and activities ramping up for the winter months.   There will be something for all ages when our rollout is complete.

- This week, that includes yearbook, high school robotics and Winter Ultimate for grades 5-8. 

 - More activities to be added in the coming weeks.  See THE WEEK AHEAD (below) for details.

 

YAY - another week of 0% positive COVID surveillance tests!
