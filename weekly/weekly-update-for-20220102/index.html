Yearbook Announcement:

Every year, the yearbook team hosts a cover competition where students submit artwork that meets the following criteria:

-         Must be done vertically

-         Done on white 8.5 by 11 in. paper, without lines

-         Cover is related to the school theme of “Spark Joy”

-         Has “Camelot Academy” written in large legible font

-         School year is written somewhere on the cover (2021-2022)

-         The deadline is January 5th 2022

-         Covers can be submitted virtually to Ms. Robin (robin@camelotacademy.org) or on paper in the yellow folder on the NHS Board.

 

 

Let’s make it a great first week of the New Year – together!  I’ll be back in touch in the next day or so with info and updates.

 

Best,

Thelma
