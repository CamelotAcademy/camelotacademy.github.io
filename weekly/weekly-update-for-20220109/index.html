Weekly Update for 01/09/2022

Sunday, January 09, 2022

BREAKING NEWS:

As of 10:15 AM we have no testing results – Monday will be virtual – read below for the rest of the week.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

Greetings!

 

Albeit not exactly as planned - we started 2022 TOGETHER - with almost no absentees and with classes fully attended and taught by their teachers. Irrespective of health, test status or geography (we had students join from out of state and out of the country) we were TOGETHER. We actually had the best attendance records of the school year last week! Thank you to every parent, student and teacher for making this connection-of-all possible.

 

CONGRATS:

-         To senior Natalie Bailey - on her acceptances to Elon University  and UNC Wilmington

-         To Instructor Ab Watkins on earning a Certificate of Completion from the Data Analytics Boot Camp at UNC

 

BEHIND THE SCENES

-         Morning Meeting each day was a GREAT way to start. On Thursday, Middle and Upper School students enjoyed sharing pets and other treasured things at home. 

-         Teachers and students alike had winning attitudes both about revisiting some things they miss from “virtual days gone by” or sharing things they DON’T miss and are glad are short-lived this time.

National Honor Society Announcement:

Camelot students and families, the National Honor Society is hosting another Service Saturday! Service Saturdays were started to encourage students to do community service every month. This week’s Service Saturday, we would like for students, parents, and alumni to visit your local park, forest, or otherwise public place, and pick up trash. Bottles, food wrappers, discarded single-use plastics, and other waste worsen our environment and the animals that inhabit it. If you would like to learn more about this event, more information is on the NHS bulletin board and in the English Google Classrooms. All work that you do this weekend contributes to our Firework service-hour counter! This Service Saturday will be held on the weekend of January 15th, but remember, you can do community service every day, and not just the weekend!
