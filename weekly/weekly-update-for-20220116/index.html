Weekly Update for 01/16/2022

Sunday, January 16, 2022

Greetings Camelot Family!

 

CONGRATULATIONS…

-to Senior Nelle Poehlman on acceptance to UNC Greensboro

-to Senior Calvin Hinkle on acceptance to Colorado School of Mines with a 10K annual scholarship

-to Junior Annabel Swansey whose art work received Honorable Mention in the 2022 Scholastic Art and Writing Awards.

 

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Most students and teachers returned to campus on Wednesday.  Others connected virtually with new hybrid routines especially designed for the unique circumstances.  Thanks to the persistence and support of all, the week went well and the rhythm was smooth.  With the help and input of parents we procured more antigen (rapid) tests, placed air filters in classrooms (including offsite locations at Yates) and measured and monitored CO2 levels to document that our protocols are providing excellent rates of air exchange in each space. We also now have three full OWL remote meeting set-ups that allow for teachers and students to have 360 video and audio conference capabilities when teacher and students are at different sites. What a testimony to teamwork and community these measures reflect!

 

 

REMIND:

 

On Tuesday we begin the second of three X Modules for the year. (The final X Module is the last two weeks of school in May.). This module means different things in different divisions:

 

-         JR MAPP has expanded farm time

-         SR MAPP has a different rotation of specials teachers

-         Middle Grades also has a different rotation of specials teachers and some Wednesday schedule fun

-         Upper School students have their X Class in the extended block:

-         For those with an X class of Civics or Environmental Science - you have received an email with a reminder of class hours unique to the X Module

-         Other students will continue with an X Class in Art (with Ms. Raleigh) in Theater (with Ms. Katie) or in PE (AM- Coach Carlos, PM - Ms. Krista).

 

 

Middle and Upper School math exams will be given during math classes this Thursday and Friday - extending the reporting period by a week.

 

ANNOUNCE:

Friday COVID testing results:

-         2 new positive from on-campus testing and 1 new positive from private testing

(None of the students who tested positive were on campus this week - thanks to the vigilance of their parents in responding to known exposures.)

-         6 results pending from Color (on-campus testing) and 10 results pending from students who tested elsewhere.

 

ALL STUDENTS COMING ON CAMPUS should:

-         Dress in layers - it will be cold in the mornings!

-         Bring their fully charged devices, with charging cords and HEADPHONES - pack up tonight! (Jr MAPP does not need any of this, Sr MAPP does not need headphone)

-         Remember masks, water bottles, planners, etc.

 

 

Students should stay home if:

-         They have an initial positive test result within the last 7 days

-         They are ill

-         They have a household member/close contact with an initial positive test result within the last 7 days.

-         They have a household member who is ill

-         They have a pending test result - either from school testing on Friday OR from independent testing done this week.

-         They have a household member with a pending result, as above (this excludes pre-medical procedure or pre-travel testing)

-         Recent travel through major hubs and/or attendance at large gatherings has created an exposure concern

-         They elect to be virtual this week.

PLEASE BE SURE THAT IF YOU ARE ELECTING FOR YOUR STUDENT TO BE VIRTUAL YOU HAVE NOTIFIED ME SO WE CAN PLAN ACCORDINGLY!

 

I hope you stay safe and warm today!

 

 

Best,

Thelma
