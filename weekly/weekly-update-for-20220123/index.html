UPCOMING:

 

SUNDAY, February 6 - From Senior Anna Bailey:

The RoboKnights are hosting another WINDOWS webinar! Last year, we launched WINDOWS to encourage young women to learn more about careers in STEM, and why taking a career in science, technology, engineering, or math is important and worthwhile. We host monthly virtual webinars with female professionals in the field who talk about what they do at their job, what it's like to be a woman in the field, and why they decided to pursue STEM.

February’s webinar will be hosted on Sunday, the 6th, at 4:00-5:00PM. We’re hosting Kaja Coraor, a software engineer at SAS who also flies her own personal plane in her free time.

I would encourage anyone who is interested to attend. Don’t dismiss it because you’re already an adult, because you’re male, or because you don’t think you’re suited to work in STEM. Hearing these women’s stories is a truly moving experience, and something that I believe everyone should hear.
