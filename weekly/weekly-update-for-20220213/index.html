BEHIND THE SCENES:

 

I wish each of you could be on campus for a day to see all that is happening - the arc of a school year is no less amazing than the miraculous milestones of babies, toddlers, children, adolescents and us all.  It seems you blink and things that were uphill challenges of a new school year have clicked - individual leaps have been made, classes have gelled, conversations and reflections are happening that are remarkable - you look around and students and teachers are deeply engaged in a quest for understanding.  Outdoors, the soundtrack has suddenly burst forth with the sounds of cheering and applause from yard games and the musicians have again started their lunchtime jam sessions.  To be sure, some of this is a response to a tease of spring-like weather - but it is more - it is community, it is the bonding that comes through holding on together through challenges, I dare say it is a JOY not to be taken for granted.
