Weekly Update for 04/10/2022

Sunday, April 10, 2022

Greetings!

 

THANK YOU Camelot Families for giving us a great return from Spring Break. Our Faculty Retreat was productive and well placed in the rhythm of the school year.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Upper School Science Communications students were inspiring in teaching lessons to our JR and SR MAPP students this week. Mr. Thomas and his class set up multiple science stations in the backyard and the youngsters loved the opportunity to interact with “new” schoolmates and stretch their scientific knowledge.   Post-class processing revealed some high school students sparked by the joys of teaching and others confirmed in their desire to seek other professions!

 

REMIND:

We have State-required standardized testing this week on Tuesday and Wednesday mornings (Thursday is a back-up day, if needed.)  Most 3rd, 5th, 6th, and 9th graders, as well as some 11th and some other students participating in State programs will take the tests in the Yates Classroom facility that we often use for Specials classes. (Second graders will test on campus the following week.) Students taking the tests are asked to arrive at Camelot by 8:15 AM both mornings (we will transport to Yates). An email with more details will be sent to testing-student families tomorrow.

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

CHESS CLUB will be canceled this Monday (tomorrow).  We will plan to hold two make-up classes on May 16 and 18, after the regular club season wraps up.
