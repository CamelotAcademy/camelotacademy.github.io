Weekly Update for 05/22/2022

Sunday, May 22, 2022

Greetings!

 

Our last full week of the school year was full indeed!  We marked the return of X-Schedule with many anticipated activities.  From Upper School/JR MAPP collaborations on fairy tales, to SR MAPP donning bee suits to explore our hives, to Upper School Poem Days, to field trips to everywhere from the Tiger Rescue to the Landfill - our students are out and about, doing and exploring. Middle Grades students have been working for weeks to create an interactive Music Wall - the installation of which was an immediate success. Meanwhile, the Civics class is hard at work on plans that incorporate feedback from an array of stakeholders alongside the reality of existing rules and ordinances. It is exhilarating to witness their process of learning from and responding to challenges.
