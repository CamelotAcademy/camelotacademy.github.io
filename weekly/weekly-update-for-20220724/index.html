Weekly Update for 07/24/2022

Sunday, July 24, 2022

Greetings and Happy New Year!

 

Welcome to the Camelot Academy ‘22-‘23 “Updates” mailing list! 

 

During the school year you will receive an email from me each Sunday morning with key information for the upcoming week.  In the meantime, I will send periodic updates with reminders and announcements.

 

We are just a month away from student return and the staff and I are hard at work on creating an amazing new year.  There’s a palpable excitement about this planning process. Teachers have been meeting all summer long in groups focused on different projects - we are excited to share these with you and our students over the coming weeks.

 

This week, our newly-launching early school (NatureStart) will run a summer program at our farm property.  This long-anticipated addition to our repertoire will bring a team of us together putting many of our ideas into action in preparation for the coming year.

 

There’s plenty for our older students as well!
