Weekly Update for 08/21/2022

Sunday, August 21, 2022

Greetings!

 

Welcome to the opening of the 2022-23 school year!  We are looking forward to welcoming new and returning families in the coming days.  Our staff has been hard at work throughout the summer and quite intensely for the past couple of weeks. We’ve been planning, visioning and anticipating this opening as it is a very special one - for many reasons - including it marks the start of Camelot’s 40th Anniversary year (with a theme of “Camelot v.1.39”)!

 

We welcome to Team Camelot Greg Kellogg (MAPP teacher) and Reed Kellner (Upper School math and science teacher).  In new and expanded roles are Betsey Cheshire (Middle/Upper School Latin) and Dan Hill (Math Coordinator/IT).  Ab Watkins has made an unanticipated move to Atlanta to provide family support - we are looking forward to collaborating on some projects long distance.

 

 

 

Here’s the plan for this exciting week:

8 AM to Noon -  PCR testing on the porch for all students and staff.

If you have children in different divisions, you may pick a level and attend only one session and request written information from the other division level/s (i.e. MAPP, Middle Grades and/or Upper School).
