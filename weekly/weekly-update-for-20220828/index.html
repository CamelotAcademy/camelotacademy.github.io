Weekly Update for 08/28/2022

Sunday, August 28, 2022

Greetings Camelot Families!

 

Do you have time for some positive news?

 

On this the 40th opening week of Camelot Academy I am struck by what makes our community tick and I want to be sure to share that with you. 

 

The people!

 

Our little corner of the world is founded on love and care and learning and celebrating both all-that-makes-us-unique and all-that-we-have-in-common.

 

King Arthur would blush with joy if he witnessed the number of bar-setting moments in the past few weeks of folks giving their ALL for a vision of what we can do together.

 

I trust you will find yourself cherishing this Team and this community and feel welcomed and encouraged to bring your own dreams and talent to the Table.

 

Behind the scenes, our opening sequence of Parent and Student Orientations were masterminded by teachers Lori, Amy, Michelle and Nicole with enormous contributions from all team members.  The sports program has been beautifully brought together by teachers/athletic directors - Michael and Bruce.
