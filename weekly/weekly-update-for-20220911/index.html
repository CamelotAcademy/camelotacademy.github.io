Weekly Update for 09/11/2022

Sunday, September 11, 2022

Greetings!

 

We welcome Luigi Rocco Glynn to the world and to our Camelot Family - the newest member of the Glynn family was born to Camille and my son Greg on Wednesday, September 7! 

 

In other new beginnings, Nature Start Early School opened on Tuesday, September 6.  We are thrilled to have this program of young people at our farm property every day.

 

Congratulations to senior Annabel Swansey who created the winning “I voted” sticker for Durham to utilize in upcoming elections.

 

Kudos to seventh grader Brooks Parker whose superior performance on the SAT- Math has earned him recognition from Johns Hopkins CTY Study of Exceptional Talent.

 

Welcome to ninth grader Yuto Chen who has arrived from China to join our student body.

 

Way to Go, Camelot Soccer Knights. The team played a strong opening game versus Fletcher Academy on Friday.

 

 

 

REMIND:

Upper School X Schedule has wrapped up for this semester. Upper School students begin their “regular” class rotations on Monday.

 

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

Gardening Club for SR MAPP/Middle Grades students starts Monday from 3-4 PM.  A few spots remain. Contact Michelle@camelotacademy.org if your student would like to visit the first meeting.
