Weekly Update for 09/25/2022

Sunday, September 25, 2022

Greetings!     This Update is PACKED with announcements and opportunities! 

 

Happy Rosh Hashanah and Happy Fall!

 

We had a productive Faculty Retreat, the highlight of which was a gathering of teachers on the porch to share immersive learning experiences conducted to date and what we - and our students - have learned from them.  It was a lovely way to share best practices and to remind us all of the important work we do connecting students to “real world” problem solving and visioning.

 

Behind the scenes:

 

It was wonderful to have the voices of so many parents and students at our “table.”  Your thoughts and feedback were an important part of our thinking and planning.

PARENT SERVICE/JOB SHADOW OPPORTUNITIES SOUGHT:

Camelot is looking for job shadow opportunities for our Upper School students during the week of Family Conferences (Oct. 10-14, 2022). If you are able to offer such an experience to our students, thank you!

Please complete the form at link below to provide more information. We will contact you to let you know if we have a student match. Even if you can offer this at a later time, please let us know!

A job shadow experience is meant to give the student a glimpse of a career or field that they may want to consider in the future. It might be for a couple of hours, several days, or anything in between. You may ask the student to participate in simple tasks or just give them the chance to observe a typical day in your career.

An important note: Offering a job shadow counts towards Parent Service hours!

This opportunity is being coordinated by Charlotte@camelotacademy.org
