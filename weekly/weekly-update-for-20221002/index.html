Announcement from the NHS:

 

Join Us For Our 6th Annual SOCKTOBER!!

 

What Is Socktober?

Socktober is a collaborative community service event in which the Camelot NHS and  Robotics Teams across the nation collect  socks and other necessities and then donate them to local homeless shelters.

How Do I Participate?

Collect socks (or other acceptable items)  during the month of October, and bring  them into school to drop off on the Camelot Porch in the bin corresponding to your grade group (MAPP, Middle, Purple  Upper, Gold Upper). There will be a prize for the group that brings in the most  donations!

Why Socks?

Socks are one of the most under-donated items to homeless shelters. Of course,  everything helps, and donations such as  the ones on the right would help as well. Our primary goal is to collect  socks, but we accept all  typical donations, including:

➔ Dental care products ➔ Shampoo/conditioner ➔ Tissues

➔ Paper Towels➔ Unscented Deodorant ➔ Lotion ➔ Disposable razors ➔ Nail products

➔ Disposable washcloths ➔ Hair combs ➔ Body wash
