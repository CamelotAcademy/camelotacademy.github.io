Weekly Update for 12/04/2022

Sunday, December 04, 2022

Greetings and Happy December!

 

Congratulations to senior Nate Hopkins on the most recent publication of his writing in Indy Week -https://indyweek.com/news/durham/hacking-into-history-durham/.

Nate writes for this publication as an intern, which is part of his senior English credit experience through our school.

 

Also written by Nate, in his capacity as an NHS officer:

Socktober is over!!! Thank you to everyone who donated socks or other household items. We managed to raise over 300 pairs of socks for our local homeless community. As a reward, the whole school will get cookies and hot chocolate!

Our candy drive was also a huge success---we collected 55 pounds of Halloween candy for local Ronald McDonald's Houses.

 

Behind the Scenes:

Thank you, Upper School parents, for your support of our early dismissal on Friday.  This allowed for a mini-retreat planning meeting for the coming semester focused around the innovative ideas being piloted with our New to Upper School students.  Look for reimagined semester reports, student-led conferences and final expositions over the coming months.

 

Remind:

The Forge Your Path Google Classroom is updated weekly. New this week:

1) Youth Ambassadors Program

WHAT: 3-week summer leadership exchange abroad in the Americas. Program is run by World Learning, and funded by the U.S. Department of State.

WHO: U.S.-citizen teens aged 15-17 with at least one semester of school remaining after the program

APPLY: Deadline is Dec. 12, 2022

Program and application details: https://www.worldlearning.org/program-pages/youth-ambassadors-program/u-s-citizens/

General info from the U.S. government: https://exchanges.state.gov/non-us/program/youth-ambassadors

2) Young Writer's Winter Workshop with The Kenyon Review

WHAT: 6-week course that meets online on Saturdays during January-February. Run by The Kenyon Review, a literary magazine. They also offer a summer workshop.

WHO: Current 10th-12th grade students and/or aged 16-18 at the time of the program

APPLY: Deadline is Dec. 15, 2022

https://kenyonreview.org/workshops/young-writers-winter-online/
