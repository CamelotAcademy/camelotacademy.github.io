Sunday, December 11, 2022

Greetings!

Behind the scenes:

The Interlude time is full of special activities.  Students have had fun dressing up for Spirit Days.  There have been many outings to local theaters and lots of especially memorable class activities.  From fencing to power tools, there is nothing “ordinary” about our days! Meanwhile, the seniors are hard at work wrapping up their January 1 college applications.  As another calendar year comes to a close, I am awestruck and honored to share the journey with our young people and their families. Not a day goes by that some conversation or act of courage unfolds right in front of me - and bedazzles me with its glow.

 

 

Remind:

 

Wednesday - Second Semester paperwork due to the office!

New this week:

1) Media Production

WHAT: 4 weeks on campus in Boston. Includes workshops on Filmmaking, Documentary Production, TV Studio Production, Visual Effects, and Podcast & Broadcast.

WHO: Rising 10th, 11th, and 12th grade students

 

2) Journalism Academy

WHAT: Around 2 weeks in length, on campus or online. Topics are  Photojournalism or News Reporting & Writing.

WHO: On campus for rising 11th & 12th grade students with some journalism experience. Online for students aged 14-18, all experience levels

 

 

Announce:

 

NHS is sponsoring a wire and bead art sale to benefit the Zimbabwean artisan Charles Masenda.  Camelot has sponsored this artwork for many years and all of the proceeds are sent directly to Charles to buy food for his family and medication for his ill mother.  Items are priced from $3 to $40 and NHS students have the items available during recess and lunch times. Sales will be until sold out or the end of the week, whichever comes first!
