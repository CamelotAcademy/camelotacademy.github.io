Weekly Update for 01/02/2023

Monday, January 02, 2023

Greetings and Happy New Year!

 

I hope the past two weeks have included some good times for you and yours.  On behalf of all of us, thank you for the outpouring of gifts and cards – it created a warmth and buoyancy that was much appreciated.

 

Congratulations to the following seniors on their recent college acceptances:

-         Dylan McGlinn - George Mason University with a 68 K Mason Recognition Award

-         Lila Stollwerk - Warren Wilson and UNC Asheville

-         Gabriella Taylor - Western Carolina University

 

 

Behind the scenes:

 

In addition to taking time away from meetings, emails and schedules these two weeks have also been a nice time to read, listen and reflect on a variety of topics - of both professional and personal interest.  One webinar of note was about the significance of Dunbar’s number (~150) in school communities. This fact followed me in my reflective sojourns - including on a podcast about Minerva University (the most selective university in the world). The relationship/educational benefits of small communities for all involved are significant and getting noticed! It was both reinforcing and inspiring to be reminded of the opportunities our size avails us in this department. 

 

As the semester comes to a close, students of all grade levels will be engaged in their own reflective exercises.  The process of reviewing and taking stock, as well as setting goals, is an important part of development for all of us.  Since the new year is full of such opportunities for people of all ages, please consider making this a topic of conversation in your home - including your own practices.

 

Over the coming weeks I’ll share a few of my own reflections, particularly as they relate to the exciting frontiers our school community is in the midst of exploring this year.
