Weekly Update for 02/05/2023

Sunday, February 05, 2023

Greetings and Happy February!

 

Much of my week was quite atypically spent off of the porch in a small back office with not much sound-proofing.  The backdrop to my report card work has been the sounds of MAPP classes and their enthusiastic days of learning and discovery.  In the midst of successively grayer, wetter and colder days this week,  KinderMAPP students could be heard excitedly counting to 100, anticipating Groundhog Day and singing beautiful songs in music class.  “Sounds of MAPP” could become a much-desired Spotify track of Background Sounds that provides a lift to winter doldrums!

 

Behind the scenes:

I have emerged from my Report Card Cocoon and look forward to sharing this beautiful body of faculty/student work with you when the Achieve Portal opens at 4 PM on Monday.  The exercise of reading each and every teacher comment is an important annual grounding exercise for me - taking stock of where we are in our journey with each student and fine-tuning goals for the spring.

 

Special thanks to Logan and Donna for their patience and attention to detail in producing these reports.

 

 

Remind:

Ms. Jill has been in her own cocoon - the Conference-Puzzle Cocoon.  It is good that she performs at Genius Level on many puzzles - and thrives on the challenge - because this annual exercise of hers is truly mind-boggling.  Please look for an email from her early this week with your conference appointment time.  Also, please be reminded that this round of conferences does not require a conference of all Middle or Upper School students.

 

 

Announce:

 

In preparation for the 23-24 School year….THREE UPCOMING OPPORTUNITIES!

I am happy to offer two Zoom meetings to provide some Q&A time with me for students preparing to move to a new division of our school next year.  We are often reminded that even families who have been with us for years can be unaware of the structure, goals and opportunities that our school provides as students move to higher grade levels.  As such, I invite you to a “kitchen chat” with me (my kitchen being my home Zoom location!) for an informal exchange on this topic.

1)     Tuesday, February 21 at 7 PM

For parents of students currently enrolled in our Middle Grades - especially those in Grade 7 or Grade 8 (who will move to Upper School next year)

2)     Tuesday, February 28 at 7 PM

For parents of students currently enrolled in SR MAPP - especially those in Grade 4 (who will move to Middle Grades next year)

 

3)     Also, if you’d like a time to come in person to chat with faculty in these divisions and explore more about how learning is structured across the curriculum, we have an OPEN HOUSE on Saturday, March 4.

(9:30 MAPP classes, 10:00 Middle Grades, 10:30 Upper Grades)
