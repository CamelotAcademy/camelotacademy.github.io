Weekly Update for 02/12/2023

Sunday, February 12, 2023

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to senior Alec Corliss on his acceptance to Drew University with an 80K Dean’s Scholarship.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Not only did the weather make an abrupt jump from winter to spring-like, but so did our project queue.  No sooner were first semester reports released than attention turned to everything from clubs, to graduation to summer camps and beyond.

Time travel to be sure!  Lots of exciting things ahead!

 

REMIND:

We look forward to seeing many families in conferences this week. Please note the correct Zoom links (see email from Ms. Jill), ensure that your Middle or Upper School student is in attendance and arrive having reviewed First Semester Reports in advance.  Reminder - please do not attend a Zoom conference from a moving vehicle or from a place with distractions or outside onlookers!

 

School is closed with no classes this Wednesday-Friday for conferences and closed Monday, February 20 for Presidents’ Day.

 

 

ANNOUNCE:

We are thrilled to announce that the Camelot Class of ‘23 will celebrate graduation with the entire school family (all students K-12 and their parents) at the Carolina Theater’s Fletcher Hall.  This marks a joyous return to our tradition of many years.  We will gather to ring out the school year on Friday, May 26 at a time TBA soon (afternoon or early evening).

 

Due to our abbreviated class schedule this week, we will not have COVID testing.  We will test on our first day back, Tuesday, February 21.

 

 

The final basketball game of the season will be:

Monday @ Emerson Waldorf at 4:00 PM

302 W. Tryon St. Hillsborough

 

Second Semester Clubs will be announced this coming week and begin the week of February 20.

 

COVID Update:

We had ONE positive case on Monday and ONE today(Sunday).

We had ZERO positive cases from our Friday COVID testing and FIVE tests pending.

Durham County CDC level is MEDIUM (weekly update as of 2/9/23.  https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/your-health/covid-by-county.html  )

 

I hope you have a great week and I look forward to seeing many of you in conferences.

 

Best,

Thelma
