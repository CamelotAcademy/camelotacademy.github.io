Weekly Update for 03/12/2023

Sunday, March 12, 2023

Greetings and Happy Snow Day!

 

Congratulations to the following seniors on their recent college acceptances:

-         Thomas Smith to Muhlenberg College with a $92,000 Presidential Merit Scholarship and Dickinson College with a $60,000 Benjamin Rush Scholarship.

-         Eli Terry to Peace College with Presidential, Academic Excellence and Community Service awards totaling $76,000.

 

 

Behind the Scenes:

The Class of ‘24 (juniors) began their post-high school planning meetings with me last week.  This is a wistful time of year for educators, as the passage of time is brought home by the next class of young people stepping up and onto the launch-pad of adulthood. Several faculty members stopped by our meeting to behold what the Class of ’24 looks like when assembled!

 

 

Remind:

 

With the return to Daylight Savings Time today we are reminded that our bodies will need the next several days to adjust. I’ve purchased a new “sunrise” alarm clock just for the occasion! Please give some thought as to how to ease the transition in your household this week.

 

We could use parent assistance in two important ways:

-         In the parking lot - PLEASE DRIVE SLOWLY - at all times (even when you are the only car, arriving a bit late!).

-         With cell phone messaging during school hours - please do not expect your student to be monitoring a cell phone at school.  If you need to relay an urgent message - please call our office (919-688-3040).
