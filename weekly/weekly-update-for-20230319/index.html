Weekly Update for 03/19/2023

Sunday, March DD, 2023

Greetings!

Congratulations to the following seniors on their recent college acceptances:

-         Alex Mastropolo to UNC Asheville with a $6000 merit scholarship

-         Thomas Smith to Franklin & Marshall College with a $120,000 merit scholarship and to American University with an $80,000 Presidential Scholarship.

Behind the scenes:
The MAPP classes are abuzz with excitement about their Academic Fair this Thursday after school.  Poster boards are everywhere and it is great fun to hear each student practicing with their teachers. 

Meanwhile, Middle Grades students are doing fancy tinkering with their Arduino projects that are quite SOLUTIONARY in scope.  In pure Camelot fashion a half-joking idea from a faculty meeting has inspired real world problem-solving.  Stay tuned to find out how the wait for a bathroom may be improved!

Announce:
The Achieve Portal will open on Friday with Mid-Semester updates. Look for an email from Ms. Jill.

In April, we will administer CTP-IV ERB tests to satisfy the state-requirement for private school students in grades 3,6,9 and 11 to take a nationally-normed test.  Many students in grades 4,5,7 and 8 will also participate.  (NOTE: these are not the same tests required of NC public schools.)

-         MAPP families will be provided their detailed info in the teacher newsletters.  Testing for MAPP students will be spread out over several Wednesday mornings in April, starting April 5th.

-         MIDDLE AND UPPER School students who are participating in the testing will do so on the afternoons of April 19, 20 and 21.  More detailed info will be sent the first week of April.  In the meantime, please avoid scheduling any appointments that would necessitate missing school on those days.
