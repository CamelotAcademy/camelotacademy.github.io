Announce:

INTERNATIONAL HOST FAMILIES NEEDED!  We are expecting new international students in the coming school year.   If your family might be interested in hosting one of these students please contact Jill@camelotacademy.org.  Host families typically provide a private bedroom, meals and transportation in exchange for a monthly stipend.  This is a wonderful opportunity for cultural exchange!

 

Plans are being made for an Upper School Dance Party on Wednesday, May 24 (the last day of classes). The event will be held at the NC Botanical Gardens from 7-9:30.  Each Upper School student may bring one guest.  Save the date and stay tuned for more details!

 

 

Forge Your Path: Upper School families should check this Google Classroom for the latest summer and school year opportunities - both local and global.  This site is a terrific resource!  Please reach out to Charlotte@camelotacademy.org with questions or if you are seeking a specific kind of opportunity that you don’t see listed.
