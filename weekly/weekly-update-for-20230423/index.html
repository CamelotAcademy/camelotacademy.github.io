Weekly Update for 04/23/2023

Sunday, April 23, 2023

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to senior Alec Corliss on his acceptances to Concordia University (Montreal) and Elon University.

 

Kudos to Ms. Lori for orchestrating a smooth and successful round of standardized testing.  She was supported by our amazing team of teachers and students who made the whole experience productive and positive.  Thank you, parents, for your flexibility and attention to all of the schedule details.

 

Behind the scenes:

These last few days were testimony to the layers and layers of individualization that go into our work.  While many took ERB tests, we also ran math intensives, provided extra help in other subjects, got advance work for Evening of the Arts completed, launched seniors in the writing of their graduation speeches - and much more.  Even the trees were trimmed to get ready for these final weeks!!
