Weekly Update for 08/20/2023

Sunday, August 20, 2023

Greetings and Happy New Year!

 

 

This is the first installment of the Weekly Update for the 23-24 school year. I hope you will find these communications a useful guide to our journey together. Please feel free to email any thoughts or questions.  I’ll do my best to respond by end-of-day Monday.

 

 

CELEBRATE:

 

 

The faculty has been hard at work for two weeks already.  We are bursting with plans and ideas to share with your family. The theme of the year is “Back to Nature” and our immersion in all-things-related will begin this week.  (Actually, OUR immersion in the topic began with last week’s storm and working without power - go, Team!)

 

 

REMIND:

 

We look forward to seeing you on Zoom this Tuesday at one of our two (required) Parent Orientation sessions (10:30 AM or 5:30 PM).  If you have not already, please refer to the email that Wendy sent on Friday afternoon to sign up for a session. Please note that NEW THIS YEAR we pre-recorded info on several topics for you to watch BEFORE your Tuesday session.  The links for that, and more detailed directions, are all in the email you received on Friday.

 

 

Student athletes (Grades 5-12) interested in fall SOCCER or CROSS COUNTRY need a parent to attend the 7 PM Athletics meeting, also on Tuesday, at the same Zoom link. Meanwhile, Wendy sent an email with all the sports information earlier this month, and that info is also posted on our website under the Parent Portal “Forms and Handout” section.

 

 

Student Orientation (required) is this Thursday and Friday, August 24-25.

-         Each division (MAPP, Middle and Upper) has special hours for these two days Details have already been sent and will be reviewed on Tuesday, along with all the other info needed for those two days. 

-         There is no before or after school programming on these two days.

 

 

Classes begin on Monday, August 28.

-         Our regular operating hours (7:30 AM- 5:30 PM) also begin on Monday.

-         Sports practices begin on Monday/Tuesday.

-         Lunch service begins this week.

-         Upper School begins its first session of X Schedule, including an after school astronomy class.

-          

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

The start of each year brings the introduction of our newest ideas.  This year we have everything from our outdoor TK/Kindergarten Nature Start program joining our campus to advanced math students taking a new Data Analytics course during the X Schedule. At Tuesday’s parent session you’ll learn about these things and much more!  (Upper School families - you will receive an email on Monday about an after-school X Astronomy elective (science class) being offered by Thomas.)

 

 

Staff Updates:

 

With grateful hearts, we wish veteran teacher Jenny Feaster all the best as she embarks on the next chapter of her teaching career with the Durham Public Schools.

 

We welcome Sarah Way to our teaching team and look forward to all she will add to our classrooms and our immersive activities.  Be sure to ask her about her hobbies/interests and what role they will play in our plans for the year!

 

Congrats to Thomas and his wife Megan on the birth of their first child, Gwen.  We have had fun getting to know Gwen over Zoom meetings this summer.

 

Congrats to Anna and her husband Sam who are expecting their second child, another girl (!), in late December.

                                                                                                                                

 

 

 

We look forward to seeing you on Zoom on Tuesday!

 

Best,

Thelma
