Weekly Update for 08/27/2023

Sunday, August 27, 2023

Greetings!

 

We had a great opening and are very excited about the year ahead!

 

Shout-out to our amazing Teaching Team - they have worked especially long hours, much of it in the heat, and all the while been “on it” in helping students feel welcome and have fun.  We’ve had whole school Morning Meetings on the lawn, launched the all-school Perimeter Project (more info to come!), run activities with multi-age groups and set the stage for a year that Notices and Celebrates Nature.

 

Behind the scenes:

Many thanks to Wendy, Jill and Logan who have been the calm and gracious ambassadors to our outreach with the rest of the world.  The delivery trucks keep coming, the phones keep ringing and this trio makes it look “easy.”  We are all very grateful.
