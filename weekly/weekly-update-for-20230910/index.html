Weekly Update for 09/10/2023

Sunday, September 10, 2023

Greetings!

 

We had an important and memorable four day week that included two days of the Upper School Gold students attending virtually and two days of the whole school contributing to our school-year Design Thinking project dubbed “The Perimeter Project.”  This project, under the leadership of Mr. Thomas, aims to have the students manage all aspects of designing and creating a perimeter buffer along the Shepherd Street side of our property. Student designs must be responsive to the dreams and concerns of all stakeholders, as well as to applicable city and neighborhood ordinances. Budgeting and timelines are all realities that are beginning to take on new meaning for the students.  It is fascinating to watch them grapple with when to estimate and when to be exact and to witness developmental milestones across the full age spectrum in their approach to the same parameters. This project is the realization of an opening-days goal to have school-wide collaboration on an interdisciplinary project that includes much “real world” problem solving. Yay, Team, for making it happen!

 

 

Behind the scenes:

 

One of my metrics of the impact of our learning experiences is when I hear students talking about ideas or projects outside of the classroom.  The Perimeter Project was definitely the buzz after school last week and by end of day Friday the first prototypes had quite literally taken shape.  We look forward to the next phases of this project and we thank Mr. Thomas for his commitment and drive to lead us into this project launch, despite illness and a myriad of practical challenges. Every member of our teaching team also contributed ideas and oversight that were critical in making this launch a success - many while also juggling an unplanned pivot to virtual instruction and/or coverage for absent team members. This kind of teamwork is Camelot’s “secret sauce” and a treasure we are all fortunate to share.
