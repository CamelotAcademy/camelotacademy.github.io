Weekly Update for 09/24/2023

Sunday, September 24, 2023

Greetings!

 

Well wishes to all those observing Yom Kippur.

 

THANK YOU, THANK YOU… for supporting our work in Faculty Retreat. The time it takes to meaningfully share and reflect on so much info - actually - is ENDLESS,  but we are grateful for the support of our families to allow us these three very full days. As you are undoubtedly aware, the teaching profession is a challenging one - our gifted and devoted team cherishes the opportunity to practice our craft with excellence.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

In addition to our faculty meetings, the building and grounds were abuzz with contractors last week - at one point representing five different trades (!) tending to everything from trees to plumbing.  A lot was accomplished in a short span of time.
