Weekly Update for 10/01/2023

Sunday, October 01, 2023

Greetings and Happy October!

 

We rang out September with fall weather, the Academic Fair launch for Upper School, and sports competitions for the Cross Country and Soccer teams.

 

Kudos to Ms. Jill - puzzle-master extraordinaire - who in just one week gathered all the parameters (over 90 family schedule requests received!) and created a K-12 Conference Schedule for the week of October 9.  You should have received your appointment day/time last evening.  Please email Jill@camelotacademy.org if you did not. 

 

Behind the scenes:

As I was putting things away Friday after school I tuned in to the rhythms of our community and noted that we had arrived at “cruising altitude.”  I’m at a loss to explain exactly WHAT registers on my screen each year at this time - but it “comes to me” when a school year has found its footing. Maybe it is the number of conversations with faculty noting classes have “gelled” and student progress is “taking off,” or that some critical number of logistics have been figured out.  Although there is ALWAYS an endless number of things to fine-tune - this school year has reached an important milestone thanks largely to the families, students and staff working so closely and diligently together.
