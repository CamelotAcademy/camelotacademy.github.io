Weekly Update for 10/29/2023

Sunday, October 29, 2023

Greetings!

 

What a lovely fall week we had - the Wednesday gathering of families on the yard for the Fall Social was a sweet highlight. Thanks, again, to Ms. Lori for orchestrating the fun.

 

 

Behind the scenes: 

 

Last Friday was mid-semester.  A quarter of the school year has passed - the reality of this passage of time is both humbling and an important opportunity for reflection.  We are proud and energized by our students - who they are as people and as scholars.  We note countless examples across the ages of them asking questions with eager curiosity and then engaging in the process of learning and exploring.  It is an exhilarating dynamic.

 

Remind:

Monday- Deadline for basketball sign-up!

Monday - 8 AM - Upper School deadline for rough draft of Academic Fair papers.

 

Announce:

 

MONDAY

-         NO GARDEN CLUB - per the published club schedule

 

TUESDAY

-         Students who wish to wear a Halloween costume on Tuesday may do so providing the costume meets school-appropriate parameters.  In addition to the guidelines of the dress code, this includes costumes with no full-face masks, no play weapons, no devils, ghosts, witches or gore.

 

FRIDAY- 8 AM

-         Students in grades 7-12 are invited to join the first meeting of Camelot's Service and Leadership Club! An NHS option will be offered for those interested, but there is no application or GPA requirement for membership in the general body. Time commitments are flexible and the only prerequisite is a desire to serve your community and hone your leadership skills. Meet upstairs at 8 AM. Pastries will be provided.

 

 

Wellness Update:

- We continue to have nothing newsworthy to report in terms of community illness - YAY!

 

I hope you have a great week.

 

Best,

Thelma

 

 

UPCOMING:

- This is a whole-school required parent event - no classes on campus
