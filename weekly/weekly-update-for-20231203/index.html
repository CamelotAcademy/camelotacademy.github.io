Weekly Update for 12/03/2023

Sunday, December 03, 2023

Greetings and Happy December!

 

Our students did a great job of settling back in after the Thanksgiving break.  Highlights included the Jr MAPP research and presentation about what may be the oldest tree on our property (a Scarlet Oak).  Upper School made great progress on moving their Perimeter Project proposals to completion.

 

 

Remind:

 

FRIDAY (December 8)

- Gold Schedule students in Data Analytics, Environmental Science and Civics - dismissed at 2 PM. 

- Gold Schedule students in other X classes dismissed at 12:30 PM

 

Announce:

 

Parent Service Opportunity!

We are looking for Parent Career Speakers to kick off Semester 2 in Upper School. These speakers will visit classes in-person or virtually. If you would be willing to visit one or more classes for 30-45 minutes, please let us know! Our goal is to schedule a speaker for each class time over two days. Here are the details:

Dates: January 16 and 17 (Tu & Wed after MLK Day)

Class Times: 9:00am, 10:00am, 11:00am, 11:30am, 1:00pm

Contact: Ms. Charlotte at charlotte@camelotacademy.org

Info you might share: how you were led to your career; general info about your career; specific info about your job position; education & training required; a typical day/week; what you like best/least; what academic skills you use most in your profession.

Wellness Update:

I hope you have a great week and Happy Hanukkah to all those who will be celebrating!

 

Best,
