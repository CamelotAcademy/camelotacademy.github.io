Weekly Update for 12/10/2023

Sunday, December 10, 2023

Greetings!

 

We have arrived at the last school week of 2023!

 

Congratulations to Senior Travis Fountain on his acceptance to Appalachian State University.

 

Behind the scenes:

It has been fun to have parent guest speakers for the MAPP classes sharing traditions of Holidays Around the World.  Meanwhile, the Middle Graders have cooked up their own kind of fun with Wednesday culinary excursions.  Upper School will end the coming week with an optional gift exchange on Thursday (Purple) /Friday (Gold) and treats for all.

 

Meanwhile, the teachers completed a reflective survey that has yielded inspiration and enthusiasm for the semesters to come.  What a gift of vision and creativity our team has set to share!   Much more to come!

 

Remind:

 

Upper School Spanish Exams begin Monday! Spanish students should be reviewing this weekend. Details can be found on Google Classroom and the Exam Guide that was sent home last week. 

 

 

Announce:

This week is All-School Spirit Week!  Everyone is invited to dress up per this line-up:

 

Monday - Camelot Spirit Day- wear a school shirt or school colors - Gold and Purple!

Tuesday - Fanatic Day- Wear your favorite Fan Attire - whatever you are a fan of - a sports team, a Broadway show, a band, a game…

Wednesday - Wacky Wednesday- mix it up!

Thursday - Ugly Sweater Day

Friday - Pajama Day

Please remember all attire must be SCHOOL APPROPRIATE!

 

MESSAGE FROM SERVICE CLUB:

This week, Camelot is bringing back its highly popular Beaded Items sale! Just like in previous years, Camelot is partnering with the talented artists Charles Masenda and his brothers from South Africa to bring you amazing gifts. Students will be able to purchase beautifully handmade beaded items just in time for the holidays. These items are things such as animal figurines, keychains with letters or tiny animals, and ornaments. A table will be set up by the lunch table on both Monday and Wednesday. This table will be running during all lunch periods for every group. Please bring cash only. Items range from $3 key chains to $40 large figures. All proceeds directly benefit the artists. We are excited about this returning Camelot tradition!

 

The Service and Leadership Club meets on Friday mornings at 8 AM upstairs in the Cafe. Students in Grades 7 to 12 are welcome to join. Students interested in joining Camelot’s chapter of the National Honor Society (Grades 10-12) may apply and submit an application on the NHS Google Classroom (code qim56bf).  Students interested in either club are invited to attend one of the next two upcoming meetings (December 15th and/or January 5th) to learn more. Contact Ms. Bev (beverly@camelotacademy.org) for more information about either opportunity.

 

 

Wellness Update:

This week we had ONE positive COVID test (faculty) on Monday morning - no known school exposure, one case of Strep, and one suspected flu case.

 

I hope your family has a wonderful Winter Break.  I’ll send the next Weekly Update in 2024!

 

Best,

Thelma
