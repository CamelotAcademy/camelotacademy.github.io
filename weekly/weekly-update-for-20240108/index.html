BEHIND THE SCENES:

Team Camelot lived up to its remarkable reputation in starting the year committed and energized in our work and also connected and steadfast in our support of each other.  Our colleagues’ lives seem especially full with family joys, heartaches and challenges right now - through it all we are there for each other - showing up with dinners and help and hugs.  My heart filled with gratitude for these wonderful people many, many times this week.  The community we create with each other is the bedrock on which our work with the students rests.

What a uniquely beautiful foundation to build on!
