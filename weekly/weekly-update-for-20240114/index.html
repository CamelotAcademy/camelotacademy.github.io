Weekly Update for 01/14/2024

Sunday, January 14, 2024

Greetings!

 

Semester One is officially a wrap!  Exciting plans ahead!

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Our Friday Teacher Workday was not only very productive in terms of moving forward on the paperwork that goes into Semester Reports, but also in planning upcoming events and summer offerings.  Stay tuned for all of those announcements.

 

REMIND:

 

School is closed tomorrow (Monday, January 15)  in observance of MLK Jr Day.

 

In the event of severe weather impacting our schedule, an email will be sent with details.

 

X Schedule begins Tuesday and runs through Friday, January 26:

 

Upper School

-         X Schedule classes: Purple 8:30-11:30 and Gold 12:30- 3:30 PM.

-         On Tuesday and Wednesday Upper School students will be attending career speaker sessions organized by Ms. Charlotte.

Middle Grades

MAPP

-          Families should consult the class newsletters for class-specific plans.
