Weekly Update for 02/25/2024

Sunday, February 25, 2024

Greetings!

 

As anticipated, we had a full and productive week coming out of conferences.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Our farm welcomed 40 chicks this week to prepare for our next round of egg production.  Jr MAPP students will soon be adding to that count when they set up their incubator and get hatching!

 

REMIND:

Monday clubs:

Dungeons and Dragons and Gardening resume

Foraging and Swifty Poetry club begin (both still accepting registration)

 

Yearbook and Vocals clubs are also still accepting registration.

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

The Apiary Club heads to the farm this Wednesday after school.  Pick up from Camelot at 5 PM.

 

The Perimeter Project building crew goes to the farm on Friday afternoon for fence building workshop.  Pick up from Camelot at usual time.

 

Summer Program sign-up is taking off!

In the very early summer (June 3-7), we offer Nature Builders to foster a favorite activity - all kinds of creative outdoor building!  Students will work with a variety of materials and skills both on campus and at our farm. This program is open to youngsters 3 to 8 years old.

 

A couple of weeks later older students (ages 8-12) can partake in Wilderness Skills, which includes an overnight camp-out to mark the summer solstice at the farm (parents welcome!).

 

You can learn more about these and all of our summer offerings here:

https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/home

 

 

Forge Your Path Google Classroom: 

Ms. Charlotte has posted many new summer opportunities for high school students.  Please check it out!

 

Wellness Update:

We had a healthier week than before the conference break - 2 positive COVID cases (one faculty, one student).

 

I hope you have a great week!

 

Best,

Thelma
