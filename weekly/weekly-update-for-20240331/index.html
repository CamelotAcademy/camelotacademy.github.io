Weekly Update for 03/31/2024

Sunday, March 31, 2024

Greetings!

 

I hope your family has been enjoying a nice Spring Break.  We look forward to resuming classes on TUESDAY.

 

Congratulations to these members of the Class of ‘24 on their recent college acceptances:

 

-         Olivia Corliss to NYU Tisch School of the Arts, Boston Conservatory at Berklee - BFA Musical Theater, Northwestern University and Pace University with a 128K President’s Scholarship.
