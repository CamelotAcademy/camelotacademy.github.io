BEHIND THE SCENES:

It is a very busy time of year! Everyone returned from Spring Break ready to jump in and be productive.  The calendar is full of trips and activities. Jr MAPP set eggs in their incubator.  KinderMAPP is expecting caterpillars on Monday.  Music, arts and performance classes are picking up the cues to be ready for the May 3 Evening of the Arts. Preparation for all of these upcoming festivities sets a joyful backdrop to the daily routines.

ANNOUNCE:

 

Host Family needed for 2024-25:

We have a male high school student in need of a local host family next school year. If you have a private bedroom with a desk, can provide 3 meals a day (and snacks), transport to/from school and a loving environment please contact Jill@camelotacademy.org . A monthly stipend will be provided.

 

 

NHS and S&LC Student Announcement:

We are excited to announce that the Camelot National Honor Society will begin accepting applications from students grades 9-11 this Friday! This Wednesday at the 12:00-12:30 (Gold) and 12:30-1:00 (Purple) lunchtimes, the NHS and Service and Leadership Club (S&LC) will be holding an information session. Students interested in joining the NHS are required to attend this session. All students who attend and meet the NHS' academic and application qualifications will be sent an invitation to apply this Friday. This application is due Friday the 26th, two weeks later. Please note the S&LC has no qualification or application requirements.
