BEHIND THE SCENES:

Our Celebrate Nature theme has had many highlights and last Monday’s solar eclipse was certainly one of them!  It was touching to witness not just the event but the genuine AWE and EXCITEMENT in viewers of all ages spread out over the school grounds.

 

Meanwhile, the school year is quickly drawing to a close.   Last week the orders for the yearbook and the school T-shirts were placed.  Perimeter Project building and many other hands-on capstone experiences are coming to fruition - exhilarating and poignant at the same time.
