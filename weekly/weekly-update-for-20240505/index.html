Weekly Update for 05/05/2024

Sunday, May 05, 2024

Greetings and Happy May!

 

Thank you for the wonderful turnout at Friday’s Evening of the Arts.  We are deeply appreciative of your time and attention to our students. Kudos to art teachers Raleigh Gardiner and Jillian Lea, music teachers Sasha Bursac and David Stemmle and theater teacher Katie Rebich, who directed the event.  Way to go, Jillian, on the T-Shirt design and Irfan and coordinating all of the tech!

 

Congrats to the newest inductees to the National Honor Society representing both this school year and next: 

Temy Mohanan-Maselko, Sophia Wong, Sam Pence, Ruby Richey, Ben Courts, Donald Hite, Cooper Terry, Maria Tsugay, Alex Morgan, Alex Bray, Luca Toth, Eva Dreps, Rayna Silberman and Risen Zhang.

 

 

Kudos to Magda Sledzik Sims for the planning and execution of the remarkable Model UN/DC Trip. Magda was supported by colleagues Tom Mitchell, Michael Berro and retired Camelot staff member Scott Mitchell. Special recognition for this State Department event was earned by students Olivia Corliss, Thomas Mampilly, Sophia Wong and Maria Tsugay.
