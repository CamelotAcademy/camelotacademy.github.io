Weekly Update for 05/12/2024

Sunday, May 12, 2024

Greetings and Happy Mother’s Day!

 

Thank you for the thoughtful tributes to our faculty over the course of Teacher Appreciation Week.  We were touched by the notes and gifts and we are very grateful for your care.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Our admin team - Wendy, Jill and Logan - are all masterful at wearing many hats and keeping calm…these last few weeks of the year ramp up to an even faster pace.  Kudos to them for managing everything from AP tests, to trip permission forms, to graduation orders and much more!

NEXT FRIDAY- May 24 - WHOLE SCHOOL GRADUATION CEREMONY- Carolina Theatre- Fletcher Hall

We can’t wait to share this event with our families, K-12! 

All students participate and it is an unparalleled way to bring our community together to celebrate the year, the milestones and the journey we have been honored to share.  This is a full faculty LABOR OF LOVE and has many components unique to this school year.  You will laugh, you may cry….thank you for making it a priority. 

-         ATTIRE for students is “nice event” clothes – please, no jeans or T-shirts.
