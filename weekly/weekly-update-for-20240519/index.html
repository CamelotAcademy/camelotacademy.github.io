Weekly Update for 05/19/2024

Sunday, May 19, 2024

Greetings!

 

Our last full week of school was filled with many sweet moments, most especially the surprise of the Senior Class gift to the school - the “little purple lending library” by the exit. Our families will certainly enjoy this little treasure in the coming school years.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Four sections of the Perimeter Project fencing were finished by the student builders and put out in the yard - MAPP students are finishing up artistic touches to be added.

 

Our baby chickens have graduated to the farm.  The MAPP classes helped them make the move on Friday.

 

Our ’23-‘24 yearbook is especially beautiful.  Kudos to student editors Mattie Ramont and Temy Mohanan-Maselko and the student team of Ben Courts, Cordi Ramont, Eva Dreps and Kai Scammell. This group, along with faculty advisor Katie Rebich, have created a keepsake the students will be thrilled to receive on Wednesday and will undoubtedly treasure for years to come.

 

The talk at our weekly faculty meeting was about how proud and impressed the teachers are with the student work coming in during these final days.  There is nothing more gratifying to this dedicated team.
