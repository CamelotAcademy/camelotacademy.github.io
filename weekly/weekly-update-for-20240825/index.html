Weekly Update for 08/25/2024

Sunday, August 25, 2024

Greetings!

 

Welcome to the first of our Sunday Updates for the 2024-25 school year!  We are very excited to welcome you and your children to school this week.

 

As you know, on Monday we will meet with all parents live via a Zoom session of your choice (10:30 AM or 5:30 PM).  Please be sure to watch the video links that Wendy sent via email on Wednesday prior to your Monday session and submit any questions using the Google Form link found in that email.  On Monday, following a brief general session, we will go to breakout rooms by division(NatureStart/MAPP, Middle Grades or Upper School)  in which you will learn more about Student Orientation and the start of the regular class schedule immediately following the Labor Day weekend.

If you have students in different divisions of the school please either have two parents sign on separately to the Zoom meeting, attend both meetings (!) or pick one division session to attend and follow up with us for info missed from the other session.

 

Here are some REMINDERS for this week:

 

Monday

- Required Parent Orientation Zoom sessions (10:30 or 5:30 PM)

- Required Zoom Sports meeting 7 PM for all parents of potential fall athletes (soccer or cross country)

 

Tuesday

- Deadline for fall sports form submission

 

Wednesday

- Student Orientation for all (note special hours)

- Upper School brings lawn chairs (item from School Supply List)

- Sports practices begin after school

 

Thursday

- Student Orientation continues (note special hours)

- The whole school will be served a pizza lunch on Thursday, feel free to send a lunch if that is your student’s preference.

 

Friday

- Student Orientation continues with half day dismissal for all (note special hours)

- Upper School brings all school supplies  

 

 

ANNOUNCE:

All students who will be bringing a cell phone or smart watch to campus need to have a parent register the device in advance using this form:

Note: Students with a medical device should note this on the form for special handling.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

 

We have had an amazing two weeks of teacher work days - among the best in memory in terms of establishing connection and purpose.  We jumped off with our theme of WONDER and then spent eight days in a mixture of professional reflections and learning, planning of classes and events and moving and reimagining our spaces.  There was much sharing, visioning and collaboration and many new bonds were formed.  I’ll elaborate a bit more when we meet on Monday.

 

Before we plunge into the year I want to convey THANKS to the folks who worked behind the sciences for much of this summer on the “backstage” part of our school.
