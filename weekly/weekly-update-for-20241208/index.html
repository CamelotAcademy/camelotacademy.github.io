Weekly Update for 12/08/2024

Sunday, December 08, 2024

Greetings!

 

As anticipated, we packed a lot into last week’s cold days! For trips, Middle and Upper School students traveled to rock climbing. Mettlesome Theater, Playmakers Theater, Bennett Place, Buehler Trail, and Bowlero!  Our Middle Grades Ultimate Team played their season opener at Voyager Academy.  Although we lost, the score was close and the team played well.  MAPP students kicked off their celebration of Holidays Around the World with parent guest speakers.  This tradition is always a highlight of the year!

The Yearbook Staff is pleased to announce Spirit Week this week:

Wednesday - Wacky Wednesday- Have a silly shirt and no where to wear it? Today is that day! Don your most outrageous, and school appropriate, duds

From NHS:

This week, Camelot NHS is holding our Beaded Items Sale! We have once again partnered with Zimbabwean artist Charles Masenda, who sells his art in Durban, South Africa. All of the proceeds from the sales will go to Charles to help him pay for his family's food, education, and medicine for his mother. We have many beautiful bead and wire art pieces that are great for Holiday Gifts! The sale will take place on Tuesday (10th), Wednesday (11th), and Thursday (12th) during lunch (12:00 - 12:30) and dismissal (2:30 - 3:40). We can accept cash and Venmo.
