BEHIND THE SCENES:

We returned on Friday minus four faculty members and several students hit hard by both GI and other flu-like maladies. We reviewed effective hand washing and other wellness routines with all students in attendance and will continue to do so.  Please reinforce these messages at home and err on the side of caution about sending students to school with any symptoms to help our community get on the other side of this wave of illness.
