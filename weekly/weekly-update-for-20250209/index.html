Camelot’s Second Annual Parent Book Club Meeting on The Anxious Generation by Jonathan Haidt, led by Ms. Amy - Tuesday, March 18, 7pm; Register here and let us know if you prefer online or in-person:  https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScTWiwjdd4MV5EAbBgRfFuFuyosuWz0qbWyXrmTwpw8wzzw1g/viewform?usp=header

 

The Anxious Generation provides strategies to foster resilience and well-being in the modern world where kids are experiencing the impact of social media, screen time and modern parenting strategies.  Connect with other parents navigating the roller coaster of raising kids and share thoughts, observations and opinions on both the book and the topics it raises.  This will be a casual, low-pressure conversation - feel free to come whether you read the book or not.
