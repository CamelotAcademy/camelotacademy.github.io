ANNOUNCE:

National Honor Society Announcement written by Alex Morgan

Hello all, As you know, Ms. Jill is at home! We hope she makes a 100% recovery. NHS recognizes that meals should be the last thing Ms. Jill and her family worry about, so we are having a food drive to alleviate some of that worry. This drive will run from Monday, February 24th, to Monday, May 19th. The link for the Signup Genius is in the doc below. When you sign up and it is your week, bring the food to school at drop-off on the Monday of that week, and an NHS representative will be there to receive the food. We are asking for the food to be: vegetarian/or not- clearly labeled (Jill is vegetarian-her husband and son are not), reheatable, multiple servings, and in containers families do not need returned to them. Please sign up to help us support a beloved member of our Camelot School Family!

 

More Details and Signup Genius  MEAL DRIVE FOR MS. JILL AND  FAMILY: https://docs.google.com/document
