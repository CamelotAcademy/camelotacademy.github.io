ANNOUNCE:

 

CONTEMPORARY DANCE- mini session- with Katie Rebich

This mini session is designed for students who are interested in learning dance, improving their coordination, and expressing themselves through movement. Whether your child is a seasoned dancer or a beginner, this opportunity will provide a fun and engaging environment for them to explore the art of choreography. The four sessions will culminate in a number to be performed at Evening of the Arts.

The session is open to Senior MAPP, Middle Grades, and Purple Upper School students. We will meet on the following four dates, from 3pm-4pm: Thursday, April 10, Thursday, April 17, Thursday, April 24, Thursday, May 1st.  Regular after school fees apply.

Participants MUST be available to perform in Evening of the Arts on Friday, May 3rd.

If you would like your child to participate, please email katie@camelotacademy.org by Friday, April 4th.

 

 

 New Summer Program: Camelot Summer Circus! 

 

Join us for a one-day, family-friendly event led by our very own Katie Rebich! On Saturday, July 26th, from 9:30 AM - 3:00 PM, the whole family can experience the thrill of circus arts—juggling, aerial silks, clowning, and more! This exciting day is designed for all ages to learn, play, and perform together in a fun and supportive environment.

Spots are limited—sign up now to secure your place!

  Link to Registration:  https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/2025SummerPrograms/CamelotSummerCircus
