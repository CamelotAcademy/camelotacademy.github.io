Weekly Update for 05/04/2025

Sunday, May 04, 2025

Greetings Camelot Family!

 

Thank you for the wonderful turnout and enthusiasm for Evening of the Arts.  I believe we created a joyful memory!  In addition to the beautiful contributions of our Arts Team - Katie, Raleigh, Robin, Sasha and David, Irfan was lovingly recording the proceedings. Raleigh’s T-Shirt creation looked WONDER- FUL as our official attire of the evening.  The WHOLE TEAM was involved in every aspect of the event - from rehearsals, to load-in to load-out.  Our students had many shining moments - and much fun. The Class of ‘25 did a lovely job as emcees and it was poignant to conclude their last Camelot event - other than their own graduation!   I hope you could “feel the love” and our gratitude to be able to share it with you.

 

Message from Irfan:

A memorable keepsake for you to enjoy for years to come!  This DVD features the complete Evening of the Arts performances that took place at the Hayti Heritage Center on May 2, 2025, along with some bonus features! A limited supply will be printed, so order soon to ensure your copy!

BEHIND THE SCENES:

We have a Lost and Found challenge - our collection has grown to four large bins of clothing.  Logan will continue his efforts this week to display it by the parking lot. Please help us reunite these items with the owners!

ANNOUNCE:

Tuesday (May 6) is NATIONAL TEACHER APPRECIATION DAY! If time allows, please send a note from you or your student to members of our Team to let them know the impact of their work.  These messages are treasured!

 

Graduation is now less than three weeks away (Friday, May 23, 5-7 PM - CarolinaTheater).  Camelot’s ceremony is UNIQUE and includes the ENTIRE PK-12 student body.  This is a parent-required event not only because everyone has a part but also because it is an important capstone experience to the year and to solidifying its purpose and intent for students of all ages. Thank you for collaborating with us in making attendance a priority. You are in for a treat - the night promises to be magical!

 

NHS Message:

Eggs and honey will be for sale on Friday at pickup.  Pre-ordered whipped honey will also be ready for pick up then.

 

 

I hope you have a great week.
