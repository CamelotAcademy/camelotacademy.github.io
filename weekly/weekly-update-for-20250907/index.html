Weekly Update for 09/07/2025

Sunday, September 07, 2025

Greetings!

 

We enjoyed an exhilarating and smooth first week of classes. Everyone has participated in figuring out routines in a way that beautifully reflects our teamwork.  I love the emphasis on trying things, observing, and adjusting.  It creates an empowering and authentic learning atmosphere for students and adults. This coming week I intend to touch base with all students again for their feedback.

UPPER School Students

On Thursday, in recognition of Patriot Day, each student will spend a half day at the Camelot Farm and the other half of the day at an off-campus service destination.  Amy Cummings sent a detailed email to Upper School parents on Friday that needs a response, please.

 

Friday marks the beginning of Upper School X Schedule.  This is the first of six week-long X rotations and runs through Friday, September 19. During this time social studies, science and elective classes drop out of the schedule and are replaced by a single, extended class that meets daily - opposite the ongoing core classes of English, math and foreign language.
