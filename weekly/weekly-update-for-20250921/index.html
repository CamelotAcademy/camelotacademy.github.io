Weekly Update for 09/21/2025

Sunday, September 21, 2025

Greetings!

 

Thank you for the wonderful turnout to Parent Night. We hope you found the experience both enjoyable and useful to your family’s Camelot experience.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

We are approaching an important “bend in the road” in the Camelot opening sequence.  With much of the “group work” of orientations, review/pre-assessment and Parent Night behind us, this week we take a deep-dive into the individualization of each student’s path for the year.  We will bring our own collection of data points - both observed and demonstrated - along with parent and student feedback to fine tune the plans during Faculty Retreat.

 

REMIND:

 

School is closed to students this Wednesday, Thursday and Friday for Faculty Retreat.

 

ANNOUNCE:

 

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

IMPORTANT:  Each family (PK-12) should fill out a Retreat Feedback Form for each Camelot student.  This info will be reviewed during our  meetings. Your feedback and input is essential to our work and is much appreciated.

 

PLEASE COMPLETE THIS SURVEY NO LATER THAN NOON on Tuesday, Sept 23.

LINK:  https://forms.gle/eXmvwt63EqCsznFn8

 

 

 

 

Academic Fair projects have launched for Upper School students.  As such, they will have a library trip Monday morning (downtown branch) and will have asynchronous work to complete during the Faculty Retreat later this week.
