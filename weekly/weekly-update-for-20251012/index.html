Weekly Update for 10/12/2025

Sunday, October 12, 2025

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to Teacher Sarah, husband Zach and big brothers Ben, Dylan, Oliver and Miles.  Ivy June joined the family on Thursday, October 9.  We are thrilled to welcome our youngest “faculty kid”!

 

Last week Upper School students had a visit with Teacher Magda and the faculty and I were joined by former admissions administrator Jill Pitino and her husband for Friday lunch. It is wonderful to welcome these treasured members of our team to campus.

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

Last Friday was a very productive day.  While many high school students took the PSAT upstairs, the faculty and I worked downstairs to prepare for family conferences this week.  We are looking forward to welcoming many families to IN PERSON conferences over the following days - the first in several years.

 

REMIND:

 

THIS WEEK IS FAMILY CONFERENCE WEEK!

 

MAPP and Middle Grades classes do not meet Monday-Wednesday.

 

Upper School classes do not meet all week.

 

Please be reminded of these important items regarding your family conference:

-Please know the conference format (in person or Zoom) that you signed up for. 

-No matter the format, please arrive a few minutes early to help us stay on time. 

-Please organize your thoughts/questions in advance to ensure we use our time together well.  We start each conference soliciting YOUR agenda items so we allocate time appropriately.

=Expect to hear from the academic teaching team about initial assessments and goals for the year.

FALL SOCIAL INFO!

 

We’re looking forward to seeing everyone at our Fall Social on Friday, October 24th from 3:00 to 4:30PM!

Here are a few details for you.

 

Entry Fee and Registration: Please register and pay the $5 per person (Ages 2+) entry fee on or before October 22nd using the link below. Included in your entry fee is pizza, apple cider, and all the fall themed performances/activities we have planned for you.

 

https://camelotacademy.jumbula.com/2025FallSocial/FallSocial2025

 

 

Location: Camelot Academy, 809 Proctor Street

 

Parent Service Hours: Earn 1 hour of service for the fall semester by registering here for a 30-minute time slot:

https://www.signupgenius.com/go/5080545A5AE2EABFB6-59497575-camelot#/

You will assist where most needed before and/or during the event. We appreciate your help!

 

Do you have a keen eye for photography? Use that same link to sign up as a photographer and help families capture memories of their time together at our photo-booth while you gain parent service credit!

 

Farm Stand Sales: Cash or VENMO (Our farm honey & fresh eggs)

 

Parking and Car Line: Please park on the side streets. You may gather to the right of the building (in front of the school vans and bus). Kindly wait until 3pm before you head to the far yard. Camelot staff or students will direct you. If you are not staying for the Fall Social, please pull into the parking lot as usual for student pick-up.

 

All families are welcome to join the fun, but please note that children must be accompanied by a parent or guardian throughout the event. There will be no aftercare this day and our school playground will be closed.

 

Also, don’t forget about our winter clothing drive! If you’re able to donate clean, gently used coats, hats, gloves, or scarves, we’ll have a collection area at the event.

 

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to reach out to our event leads, Katie (katie@camelotacademy.org) and Rachel (rachel@camelotacademy.org)
