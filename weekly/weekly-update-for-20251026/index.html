Weekly Update for 10/26/2025

Sunday, October 26, 2025

Greetings!

 

It was lovely to share a beautiful fall afternoon with so many of you last Friday at the Fall Social.  Many thanks to the parent and student volunteers and to Team Camelot for creating a backdrop for special memories.  Kudos for the leadership of Rachel, Katie and Amy S.  Can you please share your Fall Festival photos for the yearbook?  Please send to Katie@camelotacademy.org .

 

 

 

ANNOUNCE:

This week is Upper School X Schedule. 

For Gold students that means that most students who typically have afternoon early departures do NOT this week.

 

 

Fall FUN continues on Friday with Halloween festivities in many of the classes. 

Please be advised of our school costume guidelines:

 

Students are allowed to wear their school-appropriate costume to school.

Please leave all masks, face coverings and pretend weapons at home. Also, no witches,

devils, ghosts or gore.

Please also send a change of clothes if you think your student will

want to take off their costume during the day.

REMINDER:  After school program closes at 4:30 this day!

 

 

Friday is also Mid Semester. 

-         Class updates will follow Friday, November 7.

 

 

I hope you have a great week!

 

Best,

Thelma
