Weekly Update for 11/30/2025

Sunday, November 30, 2025

Greetings!

 

I hope you and your family had a great Thanksgiving week.

 

THANK YOU for the wonderful showing at our Fall Academic Fair and Friendsgiving Event.  I trust you were able to observe how much your presence meant to our students.  KUDOS to every member of Team Camelot for the united effort.  English teachers Michelle, Christine, Thomas and Amy Cummings led students to complete projects that gave us much to be curious about. Arts teachers Sasha, Katie, and Robin fueled the creative side with the delightful MAPP presentations and Irfan and his student assistants captured much for the memory reels.  Along those lines, Katie has asked that I remind you to please forward your photos from the event to Katie@camelotacademy.org for the yearbook spread.

 

 

Good News:

Our college acceptance news kicks off this week with senior Peyton Harrell gaining acceptance to UNC Asheville.  Congratulations!
