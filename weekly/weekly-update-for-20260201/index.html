WEEKLY UPDATE!  February 2, 2026

 

Greetings!

 

I hope you and your family have been able to make some nice snow memories these past few days. I could almost hear the cheers of our students from my house when the snowflakes started to fall - they went into the weekend full of anticipation of fun in the snow.

 

Congratulations to senior Lily Sperry on her recent acceptance to Elon University.

 

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

I have been spending hours each day reading every report card comment prior to their release on Monday, February 9.  What a labor of love! From Nature Start outdoor discoveries, to the retelling of MAPP student breakthroughs, Middle Grades artistic awakenings and Upper School triumphs - it is quite a body of work. The knowledge and care our instructors pour into their classes - and their efforts to share that with families - is BEAUTIFUL.  We are excited to share this with you both in the reports and in family conferences next week.
