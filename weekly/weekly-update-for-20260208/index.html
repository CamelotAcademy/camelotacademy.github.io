Weekly Update for 02/08/2026

Sunday, February 08, 2026

Greetings!

 

Congratulations to the following seniors on their recent college acceptances:

Risen Zhang- NC State College of Engineering, Stony Brook, Rutgers, and Ohio State University.

Thomas Mampilly - NC State University

Hiba Ali- NC State College of Agricultural and Life Sciences and Agnes Scott College (with a 128K Agnes Scott Visionary Scholarship)

 

Kudos to Camelot’s first-ever High School Battle of the Books team (and Coach Amy C) who traveled to Durham School of the Arts on Saturday morning.  This was a tough match-up as Durham School of the Arts’ high school team won the state championship last year. Camelot pulled out a win with a final score of 55 - 52. 

 

Way to go,  Coach Scott and our Ultimate Frisbee Team who wrapped up their season last week.  We look forward to celebrating their teamwork very soon.

 

 

BEHIND THE SCENES:

The Achieve Portal opens tomorrow (look for the email announcement from Wendy). 

Logan and my sister Donna (who manages our Achieve World) deserve applause for their great work on the grade reports and managing all of the details inherent in documenting so many “Unique Paths!”
