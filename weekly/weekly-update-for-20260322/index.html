Tuesday-Thursday - ERB testing

WHAT ARE THESE TESTS AND WHY DO WE GIVE THEM?

The State of NC requires all private schools to give a nationally-normed test.  Camelot uses the ERB because it provides the option of private school norms, which can be helpful in providing enough “ceiling” to give worthwhile info for many of our students. We are attuned to keeping the atmosphere of this testing focused, but not stressful.

 

Many thanks to Lori Hilliard who coordinates this on behalf of our school.

 

-Testing is all morning for Grades 2-10 (please avoid late arrivals and leaving for appointments).

-Please do not send sick students to school for testing.  They will not do their best and may create a distraction for others.  We will schedule make-up testing as needed.

-Nature Start and 1st graders will be on field trips
